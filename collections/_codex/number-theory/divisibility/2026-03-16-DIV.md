> {{ "DEF-DIV:Divisor" | codexify }}
>
> Let $n$, $m\in\N$. We say
>
> - $n$ **divides** $m$,
> - or $n$ is a **divisor** / **factor** of $m$,
> - or $m$ is a **multiple** of $n$,
> - or $m$ is **divisible** by $n$,
>
> written $n\mid m$, if there exists $k\in\N$ such that $m=kn$.

---

> {{ "PROP-DIV-ZERO" | codexify }}
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. $0\mid n$ for all $n\in\N$.
> 2. $n\not\mid 0$ for all $n\in\N^+$.
