> {{ "DEF-IM:Image" | codexify }}
>
> Let $R$ be a [binary relation](/codex/R). If $A\subseteq\dom R$, the **image** of $A$ under $R$ is
>
> $$ R[A] = \ran{R\restriction_A} = \{y\in\ran R:(\exists x\in A:x\,R\,y)\}. $$
>
> If $B\subseteq\ran R$, the **inverse image** / **preimage** of $B$ under $R$ is $R^{-1}[B]$.

---

> {{ "PROP-IM-EMP" | codexify }}
>
> For any binary relation $R$,
>
> $$ R[\varnothing] = \varnothing. $$
