> {{ "DEF-SYD:Symmetric Difference" | codexify }}
>
> The **symmetric difference** between $X$ and $Y$ is
>
> $$ X\mathbin{\triangle}Y = (X\smallsetminus Y)\cup(Y\smallsetminus X). $$

---

> {{ "PROP-SYD-EMP" | codexify }}
>
> For any $X$:
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. $X\mathbin{\triangle}\varnothing=X$.
> 2. $X\mathbin{\triangle}Y=\varnothing$ if and only if $X=Y$.

> {{ "PROP-SYD-COM:Commutativity" | codexify }}
>
> For any $X$ and $Y$,
>
> $$ X\mathbin{\triangle}Y=Y\mathbin{\triangle}X. $$

> {{ "PROP-SYD-ASS:Associativity" | codexify }}
>
> For any $X$, $Y$ and $Z$,
>
> $$ (X\mathbin{\triangle}Y)\mathbin{\triangle}Z=X\mathbin{\triangle}(Y\mathbin{\triangle}Z). $$

As a result:

> {{ "PROP-SYD-GRP" | codexify }}
>
> For any $X$, $(\powerset(X),\triangle)$ is an [abelian](/codex/MAG#DEF-MAG-P) [group](/codex/GRP) with neutral element $\varnothing$.

---

> {{ "PROP-SYD-DIF" | codexify }}
>
> For any $X$ and $Y$,
>
> $$ X\mathbin{\triangle}Y = (X\cup Y)\smallsetminus (X\cap Y). $$
