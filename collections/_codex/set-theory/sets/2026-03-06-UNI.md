>
> The **universal class** / **universe** is the [class](/codex/CLS) of all sets:
>
> $$ \V = \{x:x=x\}. $$

---

> {{ "PROP-UNI" | codexify }}
>
> $\V$ is a proper class.

**Proof.**{:.run-in}Since $\V=\V$, it follows that $\V\in\V$, contrary to [PROP-ZF-SLF](/codex/ZF#PROP-ZF-SLF).<qed />
