> {{ "DEF-SUB:Subset" | codexify }}
>
> {:.no-indent}
> - $X$ is a **subset** of $Y$, written $X\subseteq Y$, if
>
>   $$ \forall x :\enspace x\in X \enspace\rightarrow\enspace x\in Y. $$
> 
> - $X$ is a **proper subset** of $Y$, written $X\subset Y$, if $X\subseteq Y$ and $X\neq Y$.

---

> {{ "PROP-SUB-EMP" | codexify }}
>
> $\varnothing$ is the only set that is a subset of all sets.

---

> {{ "PROP-SUB-REF:Reflexivity" | codexify }}
>
> For any $X$,
>
> $$ X\subseteq X. $$

> {{ "PROP-SUB-TR:Transitivity" | codexify }}
>
> For any $X$, $Y$ and $Z$,
>
> $$ X\subseteq Y\land Y\subseteq Z \enspace\rightarrow\enspace X\subseteq Z. $$

> {{ "PROP-SUB-ATS:Antisymmetry" | codexify }}
>
> For any $X$ and $Y$,
>
> $$ X\subseteq Y\land Y\subseteq X \enspace\rightarrow\enspace X=Y. $$

As a result:

> {{ "PROP-SUB-PO" | codexify }}
>
> $\subseteq$ is a [partial order](/codex/PO).
