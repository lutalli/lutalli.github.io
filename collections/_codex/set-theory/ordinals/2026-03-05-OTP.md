> {{ "PROP-OTP-WO" | codexify }}
>
> Every [well-ordered set](/codex/WO) is isomorphic to a unique ordinal number.

> {{ "DEF-OTP" | codexify }}
>
> The unique ordinal number that a well-ordered set $W$ is isomorphic to is called the **order type** of $W$.
