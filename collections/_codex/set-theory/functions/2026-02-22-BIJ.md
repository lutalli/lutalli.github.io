>
> {:.no-indent}
> - A **bijection** / **one-to-one correspondence** is a [function](/codex/F) that is both an [injection](/codex/INJ) and an [surjection](/codex/SUR), i.e. an injection $f:X\to Y$ such that $\ran f=Y$.
>
> - The set of all bijections from $X$ onto $Y$ is denoted by $\bij(X,Y)$.
