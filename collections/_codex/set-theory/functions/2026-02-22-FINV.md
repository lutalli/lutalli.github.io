> {{ "DEF-FINV:Inverse Function" | codexify }}
>
> Let $f$ be a [function](/codex/F). If $f$ is [injective](/codex/INJ), then the [converse relation](/codex/RCV) $f^{-1}$ is also a function and is called the **inverse** of $f$.

---

> {{ "PROP-FINV-ID" | codexify }}
>
> If $f$ is an injection, then:
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. $f\circ f^{-1}=\id_{\ran f}$.
> 2. $f^{-1}\circ f=\id_{\dom f}$.
