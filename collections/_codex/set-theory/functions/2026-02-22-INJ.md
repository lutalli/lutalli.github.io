> {{ "DEF-INJ:Injection" | codexify }}
>
> {:.no-indent}
> - An **injection** / **one-to-one function** is a [function](/codex/F) that is [injective](/codex/RCL#DEF-RCL-T), i.e. a function $f$ such that
>
>   $$ \forall x,y\in\dom f :\enspace f(x)=f(y) \enspace\rightarrow\enspace x=y. $$
>
> - The set of all injections from $X$ to $Y$ is denoted by $\inj(X,Y)$.

---

> {{ "PROP-INJ-EMP" | codexify }}
>
> $\varnothing$ is an injection.
