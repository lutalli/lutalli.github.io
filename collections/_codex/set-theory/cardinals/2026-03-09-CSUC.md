>
> The **cardinal successor** of an [ordinal](/codex/ORD) $\alpha$, denoted by $\alpha^+$, is the least [cardinal](/codex/CA) greater than $\alpha$.
>
> The existence of $\alpha^+$ is justified by [PROP-CA-G](/codex/CA#PROP-CA-G).
