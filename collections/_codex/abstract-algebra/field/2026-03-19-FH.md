> {{ "DEF-FH:Field Homomorphism" | codexify }}
>
> A **field [homomorphism](/codex/MOR)** is a [ring homomorphism](/codex/RH) between two [fields](/codex/FLD).
>
> Namely, a field homomorphism $f:F\to K$ is both a [group homomorphism](/codex/GH) from $(R,+)$ to $(S,+)$ and a group homomorphism from $(R^\*,\cdot)$ to $(S^\*,\cdot)$.

---

> {{ "PROP-FH-A" | codexify }}
>
> Let $f:F\to K$ be a field homomorphism.
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. $f(1)=1$.
> 2. $f(0)=0$.
> 3. $f(-a)=-f(a)$ for every $a\in F$.
> 4. $f(a^{-1})=f(a)^{-1}$ for every $a\in F^\*$.
> 5. $f$ is a [monomorphism](/codex/MOR).

**Proof.**{:.run-out}

- (I): By [DEF-RH (III)](/codex/RH#DEF-RH).

- (II), (III), (IV): By [PROP-GH-A](/codex/GH#PROP-GH-A).

- (V): By (IV) and [PROP-RING-I](/codex/RING#PROP-RING-I),

  $$ \ker f = \{0\}. $$

  By [PROP-RH-MON](/codex/RH#PROP-RH-MON), $f$ is a monomorphism.<qed />
