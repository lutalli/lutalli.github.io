> {{ "DEF-SUBS:Substructure" | codexify }}
>
> Let \<structure\> be the name of a certain algebraic structure, e.g. [magma](/codex/MAG), [group](/codex/GRP).
>
> If $A$ is a \<structure\>, we say $B$ is a **sub\<structure\>** of $A$, written $B\leq A$, if
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. $B$ itself is a \<structure\>;
>
> 2. $B$ shares the same operation(s) with $A$;
>
> 3. (The underlying sets) $B\subseteq A$.
>
> If $B\subset A$, we say $B$ is a **proper sub\<structure\>** of $A$, written $B<A$.

**Example.**{:.run-in}Let $(M,\*)$ be a [monoid](/codex/MO). $(N,\*)$ is a **submonoid** of $M$ if it is a monoid and $N\subseteq M$.
