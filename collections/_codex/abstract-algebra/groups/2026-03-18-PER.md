> Let $G$ be a [finite](/codex/MORD) [group](/codex/GRP) and $a\in G$. The **period** of $a$ is the [order](/codex/MORD) of $\langle\\{a\\}\rangle$, the subgroup [generated](/codex/GEN) by $\\{a\\}$.
