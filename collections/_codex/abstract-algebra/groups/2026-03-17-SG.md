> {{ "PROP-SG-A" | codexify }}
>
> Let $G$ be a [group](/codex/GRP) and $H\subseteq G$ be non-empty. $H$ is a [subgroup](/codex/SG) as soon as
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. $ab\in H$ for all $a$, $b\in H$;
> 2. $a^{-1}\in H$ for all $a\in H$.

> {{ "PROP-SG-B" | codexify }}
>
> Let $G$ be a group and $H\subseteq G$ be non-empty. $H$ is a subgroup as soon as
>
> $$ ab^{-1}\in H $$
>
> for all $a$, $b\in H$.

> {{ "PROP-SG-C" | codexify }}
>
> Let $G$ be a finite group and $H\subseteq G$ be non-empty. $H$ is a subgroup as soon as
>
> $$ ab\in H $$
>
> for all $a$, $b\in H$.
