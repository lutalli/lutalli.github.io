> {{ "DEF-GRP:Group" | codexify }}
>
> A **group** is an [invertible](/codex/MAG#DEF-MAG-INV) [monoid](/codex/MO).

---

> {{ "PROP-GRP-UI" | codexify }}
>
> A group is [uniquely invertible](/codex/MAG#DEF-MAG-INV).

**Proof.**{:.run-in}Let $(G,\*,e)$ be a group and $x\in G$. If both $y$ and $y'$ are inverses of $x$,

$$ y = ye = y(xy') = (yx)y' = ey' = y'. $$

&#8203;<qed />

> {{ "DEF-GRP-M" | codexify }}
>
> In an [abelian](/codex/MAG#DEF-MAG-P) group $(G,+)$, we write $-a$ for the [inverse](/codex/MAG#DEF-MAG-NEU) of $a$ and
>
> $$ a-b = a+(-b). $$

---

> {{ "PROP-GRP-CAN" | codexify }}
>
> A group is [cancellative](/codex/OCL).

> {{ "PROP-GRP-LR" | codexify }}
>
> A monoid becomes a group as soon as it is [left-invertible or right-invertible](/codex/MAG#DEF-MAG-INV).

**Proof.**{:.run-in}By [PROP-MO-LR](http://localhost:4000/codex/MO#PROP-MO-LR).<qed />

> {{ "PROP-GRP-I" | codexify }}
>
> Let $G$ be a group.
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. For any $a\in G$,
>
>    $$ (a^{-1})^{-1} = a. $$
>
> 2. For any $a$, $b\in G$,
>
>    $$ (ab)^{-1} = b^{-1}a^{-1}. $$

> {{ "PROP-GRP-INV" | codexify }}
>
> If $M$ is a [monoid](/codex/MO), its [invertible subset](/codex/MAG#DEF-MAG-INV) $\inv M$ is a group.
