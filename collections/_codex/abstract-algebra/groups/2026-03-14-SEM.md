> {{ "DEF-SEM:Semigroup" | codexify }}
>
> A **semigroup** is an [associative](/codex/OCL) [magma](/codex/MAG).
>
> Namely, a semigroup is a magma $(S,\*)$ such that for all $a$, $b$, $c\in S$,
>
> $$ a(bc) = (ab)c. $$

---

> {{ "PROP-SEM-SUB" | codexify }}
>
> Let $(S,\*)$ be a semigroup. $T\subseteq S$ is a [subsemigroup](/codex/SUBS) as soon as $T$ is [closed](/codex/OCL) under $\*$.

> {{ "DEF-SEM-HOM:Semigroup Homomorphism" | codexify }}
>
> A **semigroup [homomorphism](/codex/MOR)** between two semigroups $(S,\*)$ and $(T,\cdot)$ is a [function](/codex/F) $f:S\to T$ such that for all $a$, $b\in M$,
>
> $$ f(a*b) = f(a)\cdot f(b). $$
