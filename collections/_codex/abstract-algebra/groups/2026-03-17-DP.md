> {{ "DEF-DP:Direct Product" | codexify }}
>
> Let $(G,\*)$, $(H,\cdot)$ be two [groups](/codex/GRP). The **direct product** of $G$ and $H$ is the group
>
> $$ (G\times H,\star), $$
>
> where $\star$ is a [binary operation](/codex/F) on $G\times H$ defined by
>
> $$ (g_1,h_1)\star(g_2,h_2) = (g_1*g_2,h_1\cdot h_2) $$
>
> for all $g_1$, $g_2\in G$ and $h_1$, $h_2\in H$.

---

> {{ "PROP-DP" | codexify }}
>
> Let $(G,\*)$ be a group with [neutral element](/codex/MAG#DEF-MAG-NEU) $e$ and $(H,\cdot)$ be a group with neutral element $i$. Then
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. The neutral element of $G\times H$ is $(e,i)$.
>
> 2. For every $(g,h)\in G\times H$,
>
> $$ (g,h)^{-1} = (g^{-1},h^{-1}). $$
