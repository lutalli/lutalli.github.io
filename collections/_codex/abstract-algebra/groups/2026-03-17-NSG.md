> {{ "DEF-NSG:Normal Subgroup" | codexify }}
>
> Let $(G,\*)$ be a [group](/codex/GRP) and $H$ be a [subgroup](/codex/SUBS). $H$ is **normal** if it is [closed](/codex/OCL) under [conjugation](/codex/CONJ), i.e. for any $g\in G$ and for all $h\in H$,
>
> $$ \conj_g h = ghg^{-1} \in H. $$

---

> {{ "PROP-NSG-ABEL" | codexify }}
>
> All subgroups of an [abelian](/codex/MAG#DEF-MAG-P) group are normal.
