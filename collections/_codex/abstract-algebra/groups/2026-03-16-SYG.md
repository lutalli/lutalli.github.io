> {{ "DEF-SYG:Symmetric Group" | codexify }}
>
> {:.no-indent}
> - The **symmetric group** on $X$ is the [group](/codex/GRP)
>
>   $$ \SS_X = \bij(X,X) $$
>
>   under [composition](/codex/CP). Each element of $\SS_X$ is called a **permutation** of $X$.
>
> - If $n\in\N^\*$, the **symmetric group of degree $n$** is
>
>   $$ \SS_n = \SS_{\llbracket n\rrbracket}. $$

$\SS_X$ is indeed a group:

**Proof.**{:.run-in}By [PROP-CP-FMO](/codex/CP#PROP-CP-FMO), $X^X$ is a [monoid](/codex/MO). Since

$$ \bij(X,X) = \inv X^X, $$

by [PROP-GRP-INV](/codex/GRP#PROP-GRP-INV), $\bij(X,X)$ is a group.<qed />
