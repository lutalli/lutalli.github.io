> {{ "DEF-CONJ:Conjugation" | codexify }}
> 
> Let $(G,\*)$ be a group.
>
> {:.no-indent}
> - For any $a$, $g\in G$, the **conjugation** of $a$ by $g$ is
>
>   $$ \conj_g a = gag^{-1}. $$
>
> - For any $a$, $b\in G$, $a$ and $b$ are **conjugate**, written
>
>   $$ a \stackrel{\conj}{\sim} b, $$
>
>   if there exists $g\in G$ such that
>
>   $$ \conj_g a = b. $$

---

> {{ "PROP-CONJ-EQV" | codexify }}
>
> If $(G,\*)$ is a group, $\conj_g$ is an [equivalence relation](/codex/EQV) for every $g\in G$.
