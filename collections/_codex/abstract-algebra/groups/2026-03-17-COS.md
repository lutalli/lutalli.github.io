> {{ "DEF-COS:Coset" | codexify }}
>
> Let $M$ be a [magma](/codex/GRP), $A$ be a [submagma](/codex/SUBS) and $m\in M$.
>
> {:.no-indent}
> - The **left coset** of $A$ by $m$ is
>
>   $$ mA = \{ma : a\in A\}. $$
>
> - The **right coset** of $A$ by $m$ is
>
>   $$ Am = \{am : a\in A\}. $$
>
> - We define the **quotients**
>
>   $$ \begin{align*}
        M/A &= \{mA : m\in M\}; \\
        M\backslash A &= \{Am : m\in M\}.
    \end{align*} $$
>
>   $M/A$ is also read as $M$ **mod** $A$.

---

> {{ "PROP-COS-ABEL" | codexify }}
>
> Let $M$ be an [abelian](/codex/MAG#DEF-MAG-P) magma and $A$ be a submagma. For any $m\in M$,
>
> $$ mA = Am, $$
>
> and therefore
>
> $$ M/A = M\backslash A. $$
