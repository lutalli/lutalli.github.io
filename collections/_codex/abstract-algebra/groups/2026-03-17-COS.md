> {{ "DEF-COS:Coset" | codexify }}
>
> Let $(M,\*)$ be a [magma](/codex/GRP), $N$ be a [submagma](/codex/SUBS) and $a\in M$.
>
> - The **(left) coset** of $N$ by $a$ is
>
>   $$ aN = \{an : n\in N\}. $$
>
> - The **right coset** of $N$ by $a$ is
>
>   $$ Na = \{na : n\in N\}. $$
>
> - We write
>
>   $$ \begin{align*}
        M\operatorN &= \{aN : a\in M\}; \\
        M\backslash N &= \{Na : a\in M\}.
    \end{align*} $$

---

> {{ "PROP-COS-ABEL" | codexify }}
>
> Let $(M,\*)$ be an [abelian](/codex/MAG#DEF-MAG-P) magma and $N$ be a submagma. Then for any $a\in M$,
>
> $$ aN = Na, $$
>
> and therefore
>
> $$ M/N = M\backslash N. $$
