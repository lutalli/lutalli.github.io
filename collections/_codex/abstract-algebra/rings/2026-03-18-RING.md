> {{ "DEF-RING:Ring" | codexify }}
>
> A **ring** is a structure $(R,+,\cdot)$ where
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. $(R,+)$ is an [abelian](/codex/MAG#DEF-MAG-P) [group](/codex/GRP);
> 2. $(R,\cdot)$ is a [monoid](/codex/MO);
> 3. $\cdot$ is [distributive](/codex/OCL) over $+$.
>
> {:.no-indent}
> - The [neutral element](/codex/MAG#DEF-MAG-NEU) of $(R,+)$, called the **additive neutral element**, is usually denoted by $0$.
>
> - The neutral element of $(R,\cdot)$, called the **multiplicative neutral element**, is usually denoted by $1$.

> {{ "DEF-RING-ABEL" | codexify }}
>
> A ring $(R,+,\cdot)$ is **abelian** if $(R,\cdot)$ is abelian.
