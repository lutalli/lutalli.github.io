> {{ "DEF-NN:Natural Numbers" | codexify }}
>
> {:.no-indent}
> - The set of **natural numbers** $\N$ is defined as the smallest [inductive set](/codex/IND) (see [Natural Numbers (Set Theory)](/codex/N)).
>
> - The arithmetic on $\N$ is as defined for ordinals (see [Ordinal Arithmetic](/codex/OAR)), but without any concern for limit ordinals.
>
> - As defined for ordinals, the following gives a [well-order](/codex/WO) on $\N$:
>
>   $$ n<m \enspace\leftrightarrow\enspace n\in m. $$
>
> - The set of **positive natural numbers** is
>
>   $$ \N^+ = \N\setminus\{0\}. $$
>

---

> {{ "PROP-NN-A" | codexify }}
>
> $(\N,+)$ is an [abelian](/codex/MAG#DEF-MAG-P) [monoid](/codex/MO) with neutral element $0$.

> {{ "PROP-NN-M" | codexify }}
>
> $(\N^+,\cdot)$ is an abelian monoid with neutral element $1$.
