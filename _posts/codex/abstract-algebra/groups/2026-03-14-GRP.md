> {{ "DEF-GRP:Group" | codexify }}
>
> A **group** is an [invertible](/codex/MAG#DEF-MAG-INV) [monoid](/codex/MO).
>
> Namely, a group is a [magma](/codex/MAG) $(G,\*)$ that satisfies the following properties:
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. *Associativity.* For all $a$, $b$, $c\in G$,
>
>    $$ a(bc) = (ab)c. $$
>
> 2. *Neutral Element.* There exists $e\in G$ such that for all $x\in G$,
>
>    $$ ex = xe = x. $$
>
>    By [PROP-MO-P](/codex/MO#PROP-MO-P), this $e$ is unique.
>
> 3. *Inverse.* For every $x\in G$ there exists $y\in G$ such that
>
>    $$ xy = yx = e. $$

---

> {{ "PROP-GRP-INV" | codexify }}
>
> A group is [properly invertible](/codex/MAG#DEF-MAG-INV).

**Proof.**{:.run-in}Let $(G,\*)$ be a group and $x\in G$. If both $y$ and $y'$ are inverses of $x$,

$$ y = ye = y(xy') = (yx)y' = ey' = y'. $$

&#8203;<qed />

> {{ "PROP-GRP-LR" | codexify }}
>
> A monoid is a group as soon as it is [left-invertible or right-invertible](/codex/MAG#DEF-MAG-INV).

**Proof.**{:.run-in}By [PROP-MO-LR](http://localhost:4000/codex/MO#PROP-MO-LR).<qed />
