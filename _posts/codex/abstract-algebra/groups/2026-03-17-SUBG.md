> {{ "PROP-SUBG-A" | codexify }}
>
> Let $(G,\*)$ be a [group](/codex/GRP) and $H\subseteq G$. $(H,\*)$ is a [subgroup](/codex/MAG#DEF-MAG-SUB) as soon as
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. $H\neq\varnothing$;
>
> 2. $ab\in H$ for all $a$, $b\in H$;
>
> 3. $x^{-1}\in H$ for all $x\in H$.

> {{ "PROP-SUBG-B" | codexify }}
>
> Let $(G,\*)$ be a group and $H\subseteq G$. $(H,\*)$ is a subgroup as soon as
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}

>
> 2. $ab^{-1}\in H$ for all $a$, $b\in H$.

> {{ "PROP-SUBG-C" | codexify }}
>
> Let $(G,\*)$ be a group and $H\subseteq G$. $(H,\*)$ is a subgroup as soon as
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. $H\neq\varnothing$;
>
> 2. $H$ is [finite](/codex/FIN);
>
> 3. $\*$ is [closed](/codex/OCL) in $H$.
