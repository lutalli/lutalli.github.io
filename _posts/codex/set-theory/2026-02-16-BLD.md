> <span class="math-h">DEF-BLD. Set-Builder.</span>
>
> Let $X$ be a set and $\varphi(x,p)$ be a formula with free variables among $x$ and $p$. The notation
>
> $$ \{x\in X : \varphi(x,p)\} $$
>
> denotes the unique set $Y$ such that
>
> $$ \forall x :\enspace x\in Y \enspace\Leftrightarrow\enspace x\in X\land\varphi(x,p). $$
>
> The existence of $Y$ is justified by [Separation Schema](/codex/ZF#AX-ZF-SEP) and the uniqueness is justified by [Axiom of Extensionality](/codex/ZF#AX-ZF-EXT).
