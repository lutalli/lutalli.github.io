> <span class="math-h">DEF-SYMDIF. Symmetric Difference.</span>
>
> The **symmetric difference** between two sets $X$ and $Y$ is defined as
>
> $$ X\mathbin{\triangle}Y = (X-Y)\cup(Y-X). $$

> <span class="math-h">PROP-SYMDIF-EMP.</span>
>
> For any set $X$:
> - $X\mathbin{\triangle}\varnothing=X$.
> - $X\mathbin{\triangle}X=\varnothing$.

> <span class="math-h">PROP-SYMDIF-DIF.</span>
>
> For any two sets $X$ and $Y$,
>
> $$ X\mathbin{\triangle}Y = (X\cup Y)-(X\cap Y). $$

> <span class="math-h">PROP-SYMDIF-COM. Commutativity.</span>
>
> For any two sets $X$ and $Y$,
>
> $$ X\mathbin{\triangle}Y=Y\mathbin{\triangle}X. $$

> <span class="math-h">PROP-SYMDIF-ASS. Associativity.</span>
>
> For any three sets $X$, $Y$ and $Z$,
>
> $$ (X\mathbin{\triangle}Y)\mathbin{\triangle}Z=X\mathbin{\triangle}(Y\mathbin{\triangle}Z). $$
