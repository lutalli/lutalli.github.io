> <span class="math-h">DEF-DJ. Disjoint Sets.</span>
>
> Two sets $X$ and $Y$ are **disjoint**, if $X\cap Y=\varnothing$.
>
> Let $X$ be a non-empty set. The sets from $X$ are **disjoint**, if $\bigcap X=\varnothing$.
>
> Not disjoint sets are called **joint** or **overlapping**.
