In the following, addition, multiplication and exponentiation of [ordinals](/codex/ORD) are defined using Transfinite Recursion.

> **DEF-OAR-ADD. Addition.**{:.codex-h}
>
> For all ordinals $\alpha$,
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. $\alpha+0=\alpha$;
> 2. $\alpha+\operatorname{suc}\beta=\operatorname{suc}(\alpha+\beta)$ for all ordinals $\beta$;
> 3. $\alpha+\beta=\lim_{\xi\to\beta}(\alpha+\xi)$ for all [limit ordinals](/codex/LO) $\beta$.

> **DEF-OAR-MUL. Multiplication.**{:.codex-h}
>
> For all ordinals $\alpha$,
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. $\alpha\cdot 0=\alpha$;
> 2. $\alpha\cdot\operatorname{suc}\beta=\alpha\cdot\beta+\alpha$ for all ordinals $\beta$;
> 3. $\alpha\cdot\beta=\lim_{\xi\to\beta}\alpha\cdot\xi$ for all limit ordinals $\beta$.

> **DEF-OAR-EXP. Exponentiation.**{:.codex-h}
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. $\alpha^0=1$;
> 2. $\alpha^{\operatorname{suc}\beta}=\alpha^\beta\cdot\alpha$ for all ordinals $\beta$;
> 3. $\alpha^\beta=\lim_{\xi\to\beta}\alpha^\xi$ for all limit ordinals $\beta$.
