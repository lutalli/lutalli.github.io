> **PROP-TIDC. Transfinite Induction.**{: .codex-h }
>
> Let $\varphi(\alpha)$ be a formula defined for all [ordinals](/codex/ORD). Suppose
>
> {: .par-upper-roman }
> 1. $\varphi(0)$;
>
> 2. For every ordinal $\alpha$, $\varphi(\alpha)\Rightarrow\varphi(\operatorname{suc}\alpha)$;
>
> 3. For every [limit ordinal](/codex/LO) $\alpha$,
>
>    $$ \forall\beta<\alpha:\varphi(\beta) \enspace\Rightarrow\enspace \varphi(\alpha). $$
>
> Then $\varphi(\alpha)$ holds for all ordinals.
