> **PROP-TIDC. Transfinite Induction.**{:.codex-h}
>
> Let $C$ be a [class](/codex/CLS) of [ordinals](/codex/ORD). Suppose
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. $0\in C$;
> 2. $\forall \alpha\in C:\operatorname{suc}\alpha\in C$;
> 3. For every [limit ordinal](/codex/LO) $\alpha$, $\alpha\subset C \rightarrow \alpha\in C$.
>
> Then $C=\mathbf{Ord}$.
