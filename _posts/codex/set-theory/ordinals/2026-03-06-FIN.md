> **DEF-FIN. Finite and Infinite Set.**{:.codex-h}
>
> {:.no-indent}
> - $X$ is **finite** if there is a [bijection](/codex/BIJ) from $X$ onto a [natural number](/codex/N).
>
> - $X$ is **infinite** if it is not finite.
