> **DEF-OCON. Continuous Ordinal Sequence.**{: .codex-h }
>
> Let $\langle\gamma_\xi\rangle_{\xi<\alpha}$ be an [increasing](/codex/OMOR#DEF-OMOR) [sequence](/codex/SEQ) of [ordinals](/codex/ORD). $\langle\gamma_\xi\rangle_{\xi<\alpha}$ is **continuous**, if for every [limit ordinal](/codex/LO) $\alpha$,
>
> $$ \lim_{\xi\to\alpha}\gamma_\xi = \gamma_\alpha. $$
