> **PROP-OTP-WO.**{:.codex-h}
>
> Every [well-ordered set](/codex/WO) is isomorphic to a unique ordinal number.

> **DEF-OTP.**{:.codex-h}
>
> The unique ordinal number that a well-ordered set $W$ is isomorphic to is called the **order type** of $W$, denoted by $\operatorname{ord}W$.
