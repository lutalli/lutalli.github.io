> **PROP-IDC. Induction.**{:.codex-h}
>
> Let $A$ be a subset of $\mathbb{N}$. Suppose
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. $0\in A$;
> 2. $\forall n\in A:n+1\in A$.
>
> Then $A=\mathbb{N}$.

**Proof.**{:.run-in}Otherwise, $\min\mathbb{N}\setminus A$ would be a [limit ordinal](/codex/LO), contrary to [PROP-N-LO](/codex/N#PROP-N-LO).<qed />
