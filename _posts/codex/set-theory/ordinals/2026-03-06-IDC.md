> **PROP-IDC. Induction.**{: .codex-h }
>
> Let $A$ be a subset of $\mathbb{N}$. Suppose
>
> {: .par-upper-roman }
> 1. $0\in A$;
> 2. $\forall n:n\in A\Rightarrow n+1\in A$,
>
> then $A=\mathbb{N}$.

**Proof.**{: .accent }&emsp;Otherwise, $\min\mathbb{N}\setminus A$ would be a [limit ordinal](/codex/LO), contradicting [PROP-N-LO](/codex/N#PROP-N-LO).**$\square$**{: .qed }
