> <span class="math-h">DEF-ROS. Roster Notation / Enumeration Notation ($\mathsf{ZF}$).</span>
>
> - For any $a$, $b$ and $c$, we define
>
>   $$ \{a,b,c\} = \{a,b\}\cup\{c\}. $$
>
> - For any $a$, $b$, $c$ and $d$, we define
>
>   $$ \{a,b,c,d\} = \{a,b,c\}\cup\{d\}. $$
>
> - Larger sets in this notation are defined analogously.

> <span class="math-h">PROP-ROS.</span>
>
> For any $a_1$, $\cdots$, $a_n$ ($n\geq 1$),
>
> $$ \forall x :\enspace x\in\{a_1,\cdots,a_n\} \enspace\Leftrightarrow\enspace x=a_1\lor\cdots\lor x=a_n. $$
