> <span class="math-h">DEF-ROS. Roster Notation / Enumeration Notation ($\mathsf{ZF}$).</span>
>
> For any three sets $a$, $b$ and $c$, we define
>
> $$ \{a,b,c\} = \{a,b\}\cup\{c\}. $$
>
> For any four sets $a$, $b$, $c$ and $d$, we define
>
> $$ \{a,b,c,d\} = \{a,b,c\}\cup\{d\}, $$
>
> and so on.

> <span class="math-h">PROP-ROS.</span>
>
> For any sets $a$, $b$ and $c$,
>
> $$ \forall x :\enspace x\in\{a,b,c\} \enspace\Leftrightarrow\enspace x=a\lor x=b\lor x=c. $$
>
> For any sets $a$, $b$, $c$ and $d$,
>
> $$ \forall x :\enspace x\in\{a,b,c,d\} \enspace\Leftrightarrow\enspace x=a\lor x=b\lor x=c\lor x=d, $$
>
> and so on.
