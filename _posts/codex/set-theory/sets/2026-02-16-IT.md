> **DEF-IT. Intersection.**{: .codex-h }
>
> {: .no-indent }
> - The **intersection** of $X$ is
>
>   $$ \bigcap X = \left\{x : (\forall Y\in X:x\in Y)\right\}. $$
>
>   This is a set by [Separation Schema](/codex/ZF#AX-ZF-SEP):
>
>   $$ \bigcap X \subseteq \bigcup X. $$
>
> - We define
>
>   $$ \begin{align*}
        A\cap B &= \bigcap\{A,B\}, \\
        A\cap B\cap C &= (A\cap B)\cap C, \\
        A\cap B\cap C\cap D &= (A\cap B\cap C)\cap D, \\
    \end{align*} $$
>
>   and so on.

---

> **PROP-IT-EMP.**{: .codex-h }
>
> $$ \bigcap\varnothing = \varnothing. $$

> **PROP-IT-EMP2.**{: .codex-h }
>
> For any $X$,
>
> $$ X\cap\varnothing = \varnothing. $$

> **PROP-IT-SLF.**{: .codex-h }
>
> For any $X$,
>
> $$ X\cap X = X. $$

> **PROP-IT-COM. Commutativity.**{: .codex-h }
>
> For any $A$ and $B$,
>
> $$ A\cap B = B\cap A. $$

> **PROP-IT-ASS. Associativity.**{: .codex-h }
>
> For any $A$, $B$ and $C$,
>
> $$ (A\cap B)\cap C = A\cap (B\cap C). $$
