> {{ "DEF-SG:Singleton" | codexify }}
>
> The **singleton** containing $a$, written as $\\{a\\}$, is the set $\\{a,a\\}$.

---

> {{ "PROP-SG-SLF" | codexify }}
>
> For any $X$, $X\neq\\{X\\}$.

**Proof.**{:.run-in}If $X=\\{X\\}$, $X\in X$, contrary to [PROP-ZF-SLF](/codex/ZF#PROP-ZF-SLF).<qed />
