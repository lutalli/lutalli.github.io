> **DEF-SG. Singleton.**{:.codex-h}
>
> The **singleton** containing $a$, written as $\\{a\\}$, is the set $\\{a,a\\}$.

---

> **PROP-SG-SLF.**{:.codex-h}
>
> For any $X$,
>
> $$ X \neq \{X\}. $$

**Proof.**{:.run-in}This is a consequence of [Axiom of Regularlity](/codex/ZF#AX-ZF-REG).<qed />
