> <span class="math-h">DEF-U. Union.</span>
>
> - The **union** of $X$, written as
>
>   $$ \bigcup X, $$
>
>   is the unique set $Y$ such that
>
>   $$ \forall y :\enspace y\in Y \enspace\Leftrightarrow\enspace \exists x:x\in X\land y\in x. $$
>
>   The existence of $Y$ is justified by [Axiom of Union](/codex/ZF#AX-ZF-U) and the uniqueness is justified by [Axiom of Extensionality](/codex/ZF#AX-ZF-EXT).
>
> - For any $A$ and $B$, we define
>
>   $$ A\cup B = \bigcup\{A,B\}. $$
>
>   For any $A$, $B$ and $C$, we define
>
>   $$ A\cup B\cup C = (A\cup B)\cup C, $$
>
>   and so on.

> <span class="math-h">PROP-U-EMP.</span>
>
> $$ \bigcup\varnothing = \varnothing. $$

> <span class="math-h">PROP-U-EMP2.</span>
>
> For any $X$,
>
> $$ X\cup\varnothing = X. $$

> <span class="math-h">PROP-U-SLF.</span>
>
> For any $X$,
>
> $$ X\cup X = X. $$

> <span class="math-h">PROP-U-COM. Commutativity.</span>
>
> For any $A$ and $B$,
>
> $$ A\cup B = B\cup A. $$

> <span class="math-h">PROP-U-ASS. Associativity.</span>
>
> For any $A$, $B$ and $C$,
>
> $$ (A\cup B)\cup C = A\cup (B\cup C). $$
