> <span class="math-h">DEF-P. Powerset.</span>
>
> The **power set** of $X$, denoted by $\mathcal{P}(X)$, is the unique set $Y$ such that
>
> $$ \forall y :\enspace y\in Y \enspace\Leftrightarrow\enspace y\subseteq X. $$
>
> The existence of $Y$ is justified by [Axiom of Power Set](/codex/ZF#AX-ZF-P) and the uniqueness is justified by [Axiom of Extensionality](/codex/ZF#AX-ZF-EXT).

> <span class="math-h">PROP-P-EMP.</span>
>
> $$ \mathcal{P}(\varnothing) = \{\varnothing\}. $$
