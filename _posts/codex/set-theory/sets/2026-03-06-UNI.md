> **DEF-UNI. Universal Class.**{:.codex-h}
>
> The **universal class** / **universe** is the [class](/codex/CLS) of all sets:
>
> $$ \mathbb{V} = \{x:x=x\}. $$

> **PROP-UNI.**{:.codex-h}
>
> $\mathbb{V}$ is a proper class.

**Proof.**{:.run-in}Since $\mathbb{V}=\mathbb{V}$, it follows that $\mathbb{V}\in\mathbb{V}$, contrary to [PROP-ZF-SLF](/codex/ZF#PROP-ZF-SLF).<qed />
