> **DEF-DJ. Disjoint Sets.**{:.codex-h}
>
> {:.no-indent}
> - $X$ and $Y$ are **disjoint**, if $X\cap Y=\varnothing$.
>
> - The sets from $X$ are disjoint, if $\bigcap X=\varnothing$.
>
> - Not disjoint sets are called **joint** / **overlapping**.
