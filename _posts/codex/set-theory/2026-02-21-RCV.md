> <span class="math-h">DEF-RCV. Converse Relation.</span>
>
> Let $R$ be a binary relation. The **converse** / **converse relation** of $R$ is
>
> $$ R^\intercal = \{(y,x)\in\operatorname{ran}(R)\times\operatorname{dom}(R):x\,R\,y\}. $$

> <span class="math-h">PROP-RCV-DB.</span>
>
> For any binary relation $R$,
>
> $$ (R^\intercal)^\intercal = R. $$

> <span class="math-h">PROP-RCV-SYM.</span>
>
> A binary relation $R$ is [symmetric](/codex/RP#DEF-RP) if and only if $R=R^\intercal$.
