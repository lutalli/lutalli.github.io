> <span class="math-h">DEF-RITSC.</span>
>
> Let $R$ and $S$ both be [relations](/codex/R) on $X_1$, $\cdots$, $X_n$ ($n\geq 2$). Then $R\cap S$ is also a relation on $X_1$, $\cdots$, $X_n$ and is called the **intersection relation** of $R$ and $S$.
