> **DEF-RST. Relation Restriction.**{:.codex-h}
>
> Let $R$ be a binary relation. If $A\subseteq\operatorname{dom}R$, the **left-restriction** / **restriction** of $R$ to $A$ is
>
> $$ {R\restriction_A} = \{(x,y):x\,R\,y\land x\in A\}. $$
>
> If $B\subseteq\operatorname{ran}R$, the **right-restriction** of $R$ to $B$ is
>
> $$ {R\restriction^B} = \{(x,y):x\,R\,y\land y\in B\}. $$
>
> Both $R\restriction_A$ and $R\restriction^B$ are sets by [Separation Schema](/codex/ZF#AX-ZF-SEP):
>
> $$ {R\restriction_A}\subseteq R, \quad {R\restriction^B}\subseteq R. $$

---

> **PROP-RST-EMP.**{:.codex-h}
>
> For any binary relation $R$,
>
> $$ {R\restriction_\varnothing} = {R\restriction^\varnothing} = \varnothing. $$
