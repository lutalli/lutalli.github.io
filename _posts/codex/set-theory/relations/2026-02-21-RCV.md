> {{ "DEF-RCV:Converse Relation" | codexify }}
>
> Let $R$ be a [binary relation](/codex/R). The **converse** of $R$ is
>
> $$ R^{-1} = \{(y,x):(x,y)\in R\}. $$
>
> This is a set:
>
> $$ R^{-1} \subseteq \operatorname{ran}R\times\operatorname{dom}R. $$

---

> {{ "PROP-RCV-DB" | codexify }}
>
> For any binary relation $R$,
>
> $$ (R^{-1})^{-1} = R. $$

> {{ "PROP-RCV-SYM" | codexify }}
>
> A binary relation $R$ is [symmetric](/codex/RCL) if and only if $R=R^{-1}$.
