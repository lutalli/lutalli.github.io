> <span class="math-h">DEF-RCV. Converse Relation.</span>
>
> Let $R$ be a binary relation. The **converse relation** of $R$ is
>
> $$ R^{-1} = \{(y,x)\in\operatorname{ran}R\times\operatorname{dom}R:x\,R\,y\}. $$

> <span class="math-h">PROP-RCV-DB.</span>
>
> For any binary relation $R$,
>
> $$ (R^{-1})^{-1} = R. $$

> <span class="math-h">PROP-RCV-SYM.</span>
>
> A binary relation $R$ is [symmetric](/codex/RP#DEF-RP) if and only if $R=R^{-1}$.
