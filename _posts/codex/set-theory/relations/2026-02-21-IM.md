> <span class="math-h">DEF-IM. Image.</span>
>
> Let $R$ be a [binary relation](/codex/R). If $A\subseteq\operatorname{dom}R$, the **image** of $A$ under $R$ is
>
> $$ R[A] = \operatorname{ran}{R\restriction_A} = \{y\in\operatorname{ran}R:(\exists x\in A:x\,R\,y)\}. $$
>
> If $B\subseteq\operatorname{ran}R$, the **inverse image** / **preimage** of $B$ under $R$ is $R^{-1}[B]$.

> <span class="math-h">PROP-RIM-EMP.</span>
>
> For any binary relation $R$,
>
> $$ R[\varnothing] = \varnothing. $$
