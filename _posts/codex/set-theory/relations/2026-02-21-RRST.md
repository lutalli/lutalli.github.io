> <span class="math-h">DEF-RRST. Relation Restriction.</span>
>
> Let $R$ be a binary relation. If $A\subseteq\operatorname{dom}(R)$, the **left-restriction** of $R$ to $A$ is
>
> $$ R_{|A} = \{(x,y)\in A\times\operatorname{ran}(R):x\,R\,y\}. $$
>
> If $B\subseteq\operatorname{ran}(R)$, the **right-restriction** of $R$ to $B$ is
>
> $$ R^{|B} = \{(x,y)\in\operatorname{dom}(R)\times B:x\,R\,y\}. $$
