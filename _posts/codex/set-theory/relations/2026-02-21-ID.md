> {{ "DEF-ID" | codexify }}
>
> The **identity relation** on $X$ is the [binary relation](/codex/R)
>
> $$ \id_X = \{(x,x):x\in X\}. $$
>
> Since $\id_X$ is automatically a [function](/codex/F), it is also be called the **identity function**.

---

> {{ "PROP-ID-EMP" | codexify }}
>
> $$ \id_\varnothing = \varnothing. $$
