> **DEF-ID.**{:.codex-h}
>
> The **identity relation** on $X$ is the [binary relation](/codex/R)
>
> $$ \operatorname{id}_X = \{(x,x):x\in X\}. $$
>
> Since $\operatorname{id}_X$ is automatically a [function](/codex/F), it is also be called the **identity function**.

---

> **PROP-ID-EMP.**{:.codex-h}
>
> $$ \operatorname{id}_\varnothing = \varnothing. $$
