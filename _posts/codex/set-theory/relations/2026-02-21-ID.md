> <span class="math-h">DEF-ID.</span>
>
> The **identity relation** on $X$ is the [binary relation](/codex/R)
>
> $$ \operatorname{id}_X = \{(x,y)\in X\times X:x=y\}. $$
>
> Since $\operatorname{id}_X$ is automatically a [function](/codex/F), it can also be called the **identity function**.

> <span class="math-h">PROP-ID-EMP.</span>
>
> $$ \operatorname{id}_\varnothing = \varnothing. $$
