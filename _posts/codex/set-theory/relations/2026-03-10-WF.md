> **DEF-WF. Well-Founded Relation.**{:.codex-h}
>
> A [binary relation](/codex/R) $\sim$ on $X$ is **well-founded** if every non-empty subset $A\subseteq X$ has an element $a\in A$ such that no $x\in A$ satisfies $x\sim a$.
