> **DEF-TO. Total Order.**{:.codex-h}
>
> {:.no-indent}
> - A **total order** / **linear order** is a [partial order](/codex/PO) that is strongly connected ([DEF-RP](/codex/RP)).
>
> - A **strict total order** / **strict linear order** is a [strict partial order](/codex/PO) that is connected.

---

> **PROP-TO-STO.**{:.codex-h}
>
> $\preceq$ is a total order if and only if $\prec$ is a strict total order.
