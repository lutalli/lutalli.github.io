The following theorem is equivalent to [Axiom of Choice](/codex/AC).

> **PROP-WOT. Well-Ordering Theorem.**{:.codex-h}
>
> Every set can be [well-ordered](/codex/WO), i.e. for any $X$, there exists a well-order on $X$.
