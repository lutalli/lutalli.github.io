> A **preorder** is a [binary relation](/codex/R) that is reflexive and transitive ([DEF-RP](/codex/RP#DEF-RP)).

For why there is no "strict preorder", see [PROP-PO-ASY](/codex/PO#PROP-PO-ASY).

If $\preceq$ is a preorder, then $\prec$ refers to the relation defined by

$$ x\prec y \enspace\leftrightarrow\enspace x\preceq y\land x\neq y. $$
