> <span class="math-h">DEF-PO. Partial Order.</span>
>
> - A **partial order** on $X$ is a [binary relation](/codex/R) on $X$ that is reflexive, transitive and antisymmetric ([DEF-RP](/codex/RP#DEF-RP)).
>
>   (A partial order is namely an antisymmetric [preorder](/codex/PRO).)
>
> - A **strict partial order** on $X$ is a binary relation on $X$ that is irrflexive, transitive and asymmetric.
