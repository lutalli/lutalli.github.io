> <span class="math-h">DEF-PO. Partial Order.</span>
>
> - A **partial order** is an [antisymmetric](/codex/RP#DEF-RP) [preorder](/codex/PRO), i.e. a [binary relation](/codex/R) that is reflexive, transitive and antisymmetric.
>
> - A **strict partial order** is a binary relation that is irreflexive and transitive.

> <span class="math-h">PROP-PO-SPO.</span>
>
> A strict partial order is automatically also asymmetric.

<span class="math-hs">Proof.</span>Let $(X,\prec)$ be a strictly partially ordered set. Let $x$, $y\in X$ such that $x\prec y$. If $y\prec x$, we would have $x\prec x$ by transitivity, contradicting irreflexivity.<span class="math-qed">$\square$</span>
