> <span class="math-h">DEF-UPB. Upper and Lower Bound.</span>
>
> Let $(X,\leq)$ be a [preordered set](/codex/PRO). Let $A\subseteq X$ and $a\in X$.
>
> - $a$ is an **upper bound** of $A$, if
>
>   $$ \forall x\in A : x\leq a. $$
>
> - $a$ is a **lower bound** of $A$, if
>
>   $$ \forall x\in A : a\leq x. $$
