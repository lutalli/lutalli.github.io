> <span class="math-h">DEF-TOP. Top and Bottom.</span>
>
> Let $(X,\leq)$ be a [preordered set](/codex/PRO) and let $a\in X$.
>
> - $a$ is a **top** of $X$, if
>
>   $$ \forall x\in X : x\leq a. $$
>
> - $a$ is a **bottom** of $Y$, if
>
>   $$ \forall x\in X : a\leq x. $$
