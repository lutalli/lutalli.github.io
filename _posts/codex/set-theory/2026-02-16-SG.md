> <span class="math-h">DEF-SG. Singleton.</span>
>
> The **singleton** containing $a$, written as $\\{a\\}$, is the set $\\{a,a\\}$.

> <span class="math-h">DEF-SG-SLF.</span>
>
> For any $X$,
>
> $$ X \neq \{X\}. $$

<span class="math-hs">Proof.</span>This is a consequence of [Axiom of Regularlity](/codex/ZF#AX-ZF-REG).<span class="math-qed">$\square$</span>
