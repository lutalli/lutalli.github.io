> <span class="math-h">DEF-ITSC. Intersection ($\mathsf{ZF}$).</span>
>
> The **intersection of $X$** is
>
> $$ \bigcap X = \left\{x\in\bigcup X : (\forall Y\in X:x\in Y)\right\}. $$
>
> For any $A$ and $B$, we define the **intersection of $A$ and $B$** as
>
> $$ A\cap B = \bigcap\{A,B\}. $$

> <span class="math-h">PROP-ITSC-SUB.</span>
>
> For any $X$,
>
> $$ \bigcap X \subseteq \bigcup X. $$

> <span class="math-h">PROP-ITSC-EMP.</span>
>
> $$ \bigcap\varnothing = \varnothing. $$

> <span class="math-h">PROP-ITSC-EMP2.</span>
>
> For any $X$,
>
> $$ X\cap\varnothing = \varnothing. $$

> <span class="math-h">PROP-ITSC-SLF.</span>
>
> For any $X$,
>
> $$ X\cap X = X. $$

> <span class="math-h">PROP-ITSC-COM. Commutativity.</span>
>
> For any $A$ and $B$,
>
> $$ A\cap B = B\cap A. $$

> <span class="math-h">PROP-ITSC-ASS. Associativity.</span>
>
> For any $A$, $B$ and $C$,
>
> $$ (A\cap B)\cap C = A\cap (B\cap C). $$
