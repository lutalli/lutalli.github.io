> <span class="math-h">DEF-FINV. Inverse Function.</span>
>
> Let $f$ be a [function](/codex/F). If $f$ is [injective](/codex/INJ), then the [converse relation](/codex/RCV) $f^{-1}$ is also a function and is called the **inverse** / **inverse function** of $f$. $f$ is also called **invertible**.

> <span class="math-h">PROP-FINV-ID.</span>
>
> Let $f$ be an injection. Then
>
> - $f\circ f^{-1}=\operatorname{id}_{\operatorname{ran}f}$;
>
> - $f^{-1}\circ f=\operatorname{id}_{\operatorname{dom}f}$.
