>
> {:.no-indent}
> - A **surjection** / **onto function** is a [function](/codex/F) $f:X\to Y$ that is [surjective](/codex/RCL#DEF-RCL-T), i.e. for all $y\in Y$ there is an $x\in X$ such that $f(x)=y$. In other words, $\ran f=Y$.
>
> - The set of all surjections from $X$ onto $Y$ is denoted by $\sur(X,Y)$.
