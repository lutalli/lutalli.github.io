> <span class="math-h">DEF-INJ. Injection.</span>
>
> An **injection** / **one-to-one function** is a [function](/codex/F) that is [injective](/codex/RP#DEF-RP), i.e. a function $f$ such that
>
> $$ \forall x,y\in\operatorname{dom}f :\enspace f(x)=f(y) \enspace\Rightarrow\enspace x=y. $$

> <span class="math-h">PROP-INJ-EMP.</span>
>
> $\varnothing$ is an injection.
