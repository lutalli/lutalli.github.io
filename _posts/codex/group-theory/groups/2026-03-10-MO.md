> **DEF-MO. Monoid.**{:.codex-h}
>
> A **monoid** is a structure $(M,*)$, where $M$ is a set and $\*$ is a [binary operator](/codex/F) on $M$, that satisfies the following properties:
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. $\*$ is associative: For any $x$, $y$ and $z\in M$,
>
>    $$ x*(y*z) = (x*y)*z. $$
>
> 2. There exists a **neutral element** $e\in M$ such that for every $x\in M$,
>
>    $$ x*e = e*x = x. $$

> **PROP-MO-NEU.**{:.codex-h}
>
> The neutral element of a monoid is unique.

**Proof.**{:.run-in}If both $e$ and $e'$ are neutral elements,

$$ e = e*e' = e'. $$
