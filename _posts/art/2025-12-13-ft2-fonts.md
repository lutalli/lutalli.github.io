I couldn't find any usable [Fasttracker II](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/FastTracker_2) fonts online, so I made my own. Fasttracker II uses more than two different typefaces, but for now I've only ported #1 and #2, which are used in the program's *Help* screen. I manually drew these fonts using [FontStruct](https://fontstruct.com/), based on the BMP images `font1.bmp` and `font2.bmp` I found in [8bitbubsy/ft2-clone](https://github.com/8bitbubsy/ft2-clone/tree/master/src/gfxdata/bmp).
