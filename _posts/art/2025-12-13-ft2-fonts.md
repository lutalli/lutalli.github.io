I couldn't find any Fasttracker II font available in TTF or OTF format on the internet (except for one that doesn't include uppercase letters), so I made my own. Fasttracker II uses more than two different typefaces, and at the moment I've only ported #1 and #2 - the two used in the "Help" section in the program. They were created with [FontStruct](https://fontstruct.com/), using the BMP images `font1.bmp` and `font2.bmp` from [8bitbubsy/ft2-clone](https://github.com/8bitbubsy/ft2-clone/tree/master/src/gfxdata/bmp) as references.
