>
> Let $X$ be a non-empty set and $Y$ be a normed space. The following defines a norm on $\BMap(X,Y)$, called the **supremum norm**:
>
> $$ \lVert f\rVert_\sup = \sup\{\lVert f(x)\rVert : x\in X\}. $$
