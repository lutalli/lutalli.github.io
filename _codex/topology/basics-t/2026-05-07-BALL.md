> {{ "BALL#DEF:Ball" | cod }}
>
> Let $X$ be a metric space, $a\in X$ and $r\in\R^+$.
>
> {:.no-indent}
> - The **open ball** of radius $r$ around $a$ is
>
>   $$ \mathcal{B}_r(a) = \{x\in X : d(a,x)<r\}. $$
>
> - The **closed ball** of radius $r$ around $a$ is
>
>   $$ \mathcal{B}^\bullet_r(a) = \{x\in X : d(a,x)\leq r\}. $$
