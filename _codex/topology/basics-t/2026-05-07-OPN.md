> {{ "OPN#DEF:Open Set" | cod }}
>
> Let $X$ be a metric space and $Y\subseteq X$. $Y$ is **open** if
>
> $$ Y = \inter Y. $$

---

> {{ "OPN#PROP-U" | cod }}
>
> Let $X$ be a metric space. If $\mathcal{S}$ is a collection of open sets from $X$, then $\bigcap\mathcal{S}$ is open.
