> {{ "BMAP#DEF:Bounded Mapping" | cod }}
>
> Let $X$ be a non-empty set and $Y$ be a metric space. A mapping $f:X\to Y$ is **bounded** if $\ran f$ is bounded.
>
> We write
>
> $$ \BMap(X,Y) = \{f\in\Map(X,Y) : \text{$f$ is bounded}\}. $$
