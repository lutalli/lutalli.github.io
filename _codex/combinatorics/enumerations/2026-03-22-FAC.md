> {{ "FAC#DEF:Factorial" | cod }}
>
> The **factorial** of $n\in\N$, written $n!$, is defined recursively:
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. $$ 0! = 1. $$
>
> 2. For all $n\in\N^+$,
>
>    $$ n! = (n-1)!\cdot n. $$
>
> For $n\in\N^+$ we have
>
> $$ n! = \prod_{k=1}^n k. $$
