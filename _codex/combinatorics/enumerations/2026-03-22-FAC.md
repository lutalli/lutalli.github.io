> {{ "DEF-FAC:Factorial" | codexify }}
>
> The **factorial** of $n\in\N$, written $n!$, is defined recursively:
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. $0! = 1$;
> 2. $n! = (n-1)!\cdot n$ for all $n\in\N^+$.
>
> For $n\in\N^+$ we have
>
> $$ n! = \prod_{k=1}^n k. $$
