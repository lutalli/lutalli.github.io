> {{ "DEF-RR:Real Numbers" | codexify }}
>
> The **real number system** $(\R,+,\cdot,0,1,\leq)$ is, up to [isomorphism](/codex/FH), the unique [ordered field](/codex/OF) that is [Dedekind-complete](/codex/DKCP).
>
> The existence of such a field can be shown through construction. See [Construction of the real numbers (Wikipedia)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Construction_of_the_real_numbers) and its references.
>
> Based on the construction method, $\Q$ can be embedded into $\R$. Then we can say
>
> $$ \N \subseteq \Z \subseteq \Q \subseteq \R. $$
