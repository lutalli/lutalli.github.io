> {{ "IVB#DEF:Iverson Brackets" | cod }}
>
> If $\varphi$ is a statement, we define
>
> $$ [\varphi] = \begin{cases}
      1, & \text{if $\varphi$ is true}; \\
      0, & \text{if $\varphi$ is false},
  \end{cases} $$
>
> where the meaning of $1$ and $0$ should be clear in the given context (usually they are the natural numbers $0$ and $1$).
