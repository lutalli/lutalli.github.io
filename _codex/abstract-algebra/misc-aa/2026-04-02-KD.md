> {{ "KD#DEF:Kronecker Delta" | cod }}
>
> For any $i$ and $j$, we write
>
> $$ \delta_{i,j} = \begin{cases}
      1, & \text{if $i=j$}, \\
      0, & \text{if $i\neq j$},
  \end{cases} $$
>
> where the meaning of $1$ and $0$ should be clear in the given context (usually they are the natural numbers $0$ and $1$).
>
> Using Iverson Brackets we have
>
> $$ \delta_{i,j} = [i=j]. $$
