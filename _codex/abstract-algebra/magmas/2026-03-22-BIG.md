> {{ "DEF-BIG:Sequential Operation" | codexify }}
>
> Suppose
>
> - $(M,\*)$ is a [magma](/codex/MAG);
> - $a$, $b\in\Z$ with $a\leq b$;
> - $F:\Z\to M$ is a [function](/codex/F).
>
> We define
>
> $$ \prod_{k=a}^b F(k) $$
>
> recursively:
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. $$ \prod_{k=a}^a F(k) = F(a). $$
>
> 2. For $b\geq a+1$,
>
>    $$ \prod_{k=a}^b F(k) = \left[\prod_{k=a}^{b-1} F(k)\right]*F(b). $$
>
> If $\*$ denotes addition, we use the symbol
>
> $$ \sum_{k=a}^b F(k) $$
>
> instead.
