>
> A **monoid [homomorphism](/codex/MOR)** between two monoids $(M,\*,e)$ and $(N,\diamond,i)$ is a [function](/codex/F) $f:M\to N$ such that:
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. For all $a$, $b\in M$,
>
>    $$ f(a*b) = f(a)\diamond f(b). $$
>
> 2. $$ f(e) = i. $$
