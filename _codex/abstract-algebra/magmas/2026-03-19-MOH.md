> {{ "MOH#DEF:Monoid Homomorphism" | cod }}
>
> A **monoid homomorphism** between two monoids $M$ and $N$ is a function $f:M\to N$ such that:
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. For all $a$, $b\in M$,
>
>    $$ f(ab) = f(a)f(b). $$
>
> 2. If $e$ and $i$ are the neutral elements of $M$ and $N$ respectively,
>
>    $$ f(e) = i. $$
