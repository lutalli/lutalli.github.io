> {{ "MORD#DEF:Order" | cod }}
>
> {:.no-indent}
> - The **order** of a magma $M$ is the cardinality of its underlying set $M$, denoted by $\lvert M\rvert$.
>
> - A magma is **finite** if its order is a natural number. Otherwise it is **infinite**.
