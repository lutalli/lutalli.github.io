> {{ "DEF-MORD:Order" | codexify }}
>
> {:.no-indent}
> - The **order** of a [magma](/codex/MAG) $(M,\*)$ is the [cardinality](/codex/CARD) of its underlying set $M$, denoted by $\lvert M\rvert$.
>
> - A magma is **finite** if its order is a [natural number](/codex/N). Otherwise it is **infinite**.
