> {{ "SF#DEF:Subfield" | cod }}
>
> Let $(F,+,\cdot)$ be a field. $(K,+,\cdot)$ is a **subfield** of $F$ if it is a field and $K\subseteq F$.

---

> {{ "PROP-SF-A" | codexify }}
>
> Let $F$ be a field. $K$ is a subfield of $F$ as soon as:
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. $K$ is a subring.
>
> 2. *Closure under inversion.* For all $a\in K\setminus\\{0\\}$,
>
>    $$ a^{-1}\in K. $$
