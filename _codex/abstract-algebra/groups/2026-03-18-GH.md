> {{ "DEF-GH:Group Homomorphism" | codexify }}
>
> A **group [homomorphism](/codex/MOR)** between two [groups](/codex/GRP) $(G,\*)$ and $(H,\diamond)$ is a [function](/codex/F) $f:G\to H$ such that for all $a$, $b\in G$,
>
> $$ f(a*b) = f(a)\diamond f(b). $$

> {{ "DEF-GH-KER:Kernel" | codexify }}
>
> The **kernel** of a group homomorphism $f:G\to H$ is
>
> $$ \ker f = f^{-1}\big[\{i\}\big], $$
>
> where $i$ is the neutral element of $H$.

---

> {{ "PROP-GH-A" | codexify }}
>
> Let $f:G\to H$ be a group homomorphism, $e$ be the neutral element of $G$ and $i$ be the neutral element of $H$.
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. $f(e)=i$.
> 2. $f(a^{-1})=f(a)^{-1}$ for every $a\in G$.

> {{ "PROP-GH-KER" | codexify }}
>
> Let $f:G\to H$ be a group homomorphism. $\ker f$ is a [normal subgroup](/codex/NSG) of $G$.

> {{ "PROP-GH-IM" | codexify }}
>
> Let $f:G\to H$ be a group homomorphism. $\im f$ is a [subgroup](/codex/SUBS) of $H$.

---

> {{ "PROP-GH-K" | codexify }}
>
> Let $f:G\to H$ be a group homomorphism. For any $a$, $b\in G$,
>
> $$ f(a) = f(b) \enspace\leftrightarrow\enspace ab^{-1}\in\ker f. $$

As a corollary:

> {{ "PROP-GH-MON" | codexify }}
>
> Let $f:G\to H$ be a group homomorphism and $e$ be the neutral element of $G$. $f$ is a [monomorphism](/codex/MOR) if and only if
>
> $$ \ker f = \{e\}. $$
