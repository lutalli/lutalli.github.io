> {{ "PROP-GEN:Generated Subgroup" | codexify }}
>
> Let $G$ be a [group](/codex/GRP) and $S\subseteq G$ be a subset.
>
> {:.no-indent}
> - The **subgroup generated by $S$** is the minimal [subgroup](/codex/SUBS) that contains $S$:
>
>   $$ \langle S\rangle = \bigcap\{H\leq G:S\subseteq H\}. $$
>
> - If $H$ is a subgroup and $\langle S\rangle=H$, we say $S$ **generates** $H$. The elements of $S$ are called **generators**.

---

> {{ "PROP-GEN-TRI" | codexify }}
>
> Let $G$ be a group with neutral element $e$.
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. $\langle\varnothing\rangle = \\{e\\}$.
> 2. $\langle\\{e\\}\rangle = G$.
