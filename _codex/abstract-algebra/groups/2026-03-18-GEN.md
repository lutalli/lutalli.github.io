> {{ "GEN#PROP:Generated Subgroup" | cod }}
>
> Let $G$ be a group and $S\subseteq G$ be a subset.
>
> {:.no-indent}
> - The **subgroup generated by $S$** is the minimal subgroup that contains $S$:
>
>   $$ \langle S\rangle = \bigcap\{H\leq G:S\subseteq H\}. $$
>
> - If $H$ is a subgroup and $\langle S\rangle=H$, we say $S$ **generates** $H$. The elements of $S$ are then called **generators**.

---

> {{ "GEN#PROP-TRI" | cod }}
>
> Let $G$ be a group with neutral element $e$.
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. $$ \langle\varnothing\rangle = \{e\}. $$
>
> 2. $$ \langle\{e\}\rangle = G. $$
