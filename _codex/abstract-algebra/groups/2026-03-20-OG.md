> {{ "DEF-OG:Ordered Group" | codexify }}
>
> {:.no-indent}
> - Let $G$ be a group and $\leq$ be a partial order. $G$ is **partially ordered by $\leq$** if for any $a$, $b$, $x\in G$,
>
>   $$ a\leq b \enspace\rimp\enspace ax\leq bx \,\land\, xa\leq xb. $$
>
> - $G$ is **totally ordered** if $\leq$ is total.
