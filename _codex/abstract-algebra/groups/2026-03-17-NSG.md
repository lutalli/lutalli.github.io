> {{ "NSG#DEF:Normal Subgroup" | cod }}
>
> Let $G$ be a group and $H$ be a subgroup. $H$ is **normal** if it is closed under conjugation, i.e. for any $g\in G$ and for all $a\in H$,
>
> $$ \conj_g a = gag^{-1} \in H. $$

---

> {{ "NSG#PROP-ABEL" | cod }}
>
> All subgroups of an abelian group are normal.
