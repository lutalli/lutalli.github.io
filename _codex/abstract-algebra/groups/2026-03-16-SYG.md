> {{ "SYG#DEF:Symmetric Group" | cod }}
>
> {:.no-indent}
> - The **symmetric group** on $X$ is the group
>
>   $$ \SS_X = \bij(X,X) $$
>
>   equipped with composition. Each element of $\SS_X$ is called a **permutation** of $X$.
>
> - If $n\in\N^+$, the **symmetric group of degree $n$** is
>
>   $$ \SS_n = \SS_{\llbra n\rrbra}. $$

$\SS_X$ is indeed a group:

**Proof.**{:.run-in}By [CP#PROP-FMO](/codex/CP#PROP-FMO), $\fun(X,X)$ is a monoid. Since

$$ \bij(X,X) = \inv\fun(X,X), $$

$\bij(X,X)$ is a group by [GRP#PROP-INV](/codex/GRP#PROP-INV).<qed />
