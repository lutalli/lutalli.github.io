> {{ "ZD#DEF:Zero Divisor" | cod }}
>
> Let $R$ be a ring and $a\in R$.
>
> {:.no-indent}
> - $a$ is a **left zero divisor** if there exists $x\in R\setminus\\{0\\}$ such that
>
>   $$ ax = 0. $$
>
> - $a$ is a **right zero divisor** if there exists $x\in R\setminus\\{0\\}$ such that
>
>   $$ xa = 0. $$
>
> - $a$ is a **zero divisor** if it is a left zero divisor or right zero divisor.
