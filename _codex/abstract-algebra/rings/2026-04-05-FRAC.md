> {{ "FRAC#DEF:Fraction Field" | cod }}
>
> Let $R$ be an integral domain and $R^\*=R\setminus\\{0\\}$. Define the equivalence relation on $R\times R^\*$:
>
> $$ (a,b)\sim(x,y) \enspace\lrimp\enspace ax=by $$
>
> for all $(a,b)$, $(x,y)\in R\times R^*$; and write
>
> $$ \frac{a}{b} = [(a,b)] $$
>
> for all $(a,b)\in R\times R^*$.
>
> The **fraction field** of $R$ is the field
>
> $$ \fract R = (R\times R^*)/{\sim} $$
>
> with addition and multiplication defined by
>
> $$ \begin{align*}
      \frac{a}{b} + \frac{x}{y} &= \frac{ay+bx}{by}, \\
      \frac{a}{b}\cdot\frac{x}{y} &= \frac{ax}{by}
  \end{align*} $$
>
> for all $a/b$, $x/y\in\fract R.$
