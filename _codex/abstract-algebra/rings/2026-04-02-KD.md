> {{ "DEF-KD:Kronecker Delta" | codexify }}
>
> For any $i$ and $j$, we write
>
> $$ \delta_{i,j} = \begin{cases}
      1, & \text{if $i=j$}, \\
      0, & \text{if $i\neq j$},
  \end{cases} $$
>
> where the meaning of $1$ and $0$ should be clear in the given context.
>
> Using [Iverson Brackets](/codex/IVB) we have
>
> $$ \delta_{i,j} = [i=j]. $$
