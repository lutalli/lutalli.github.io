> {{ "PROP-SR-A" | codexify }}
>
> Let $(R,+,\cdot,0,1)$ be a [ring](/codex/RING). $S$ is a [subring](/codex/SUBS) as soon as
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. $a-b\in S$ for all $a$, $b\in S$;
> 2. $1\in S$;
> 3. $ab\in S$ for all $a$, $b\in S$.
>
> (I) guarantees that $(R,+,0)$ is a [subgroup](/codex/SG#PROP-SG-B); (II) and (III) guarantee that $(R,\cdot,1)$ is a [submonoid](/codex/SMO#PROP-SMO-A) with the same neutral element.
