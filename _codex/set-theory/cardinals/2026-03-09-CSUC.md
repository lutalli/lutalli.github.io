> {{ "DEF-CSUC:Cardinal Successor" | codexify }}
>
> The **cardinal successor** of an ordinal $\alpha$, denoted by $\alpha^+$, is the least cardinal greater than $\alpha$.
>
> The existence of $\alpha^+$ is justified by [PROP-CA-G](/codex/CA#PROP-CA-G).
