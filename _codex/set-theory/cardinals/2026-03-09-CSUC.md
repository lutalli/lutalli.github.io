> {{ "CSUC#DEF:Cardinal Successor" | cod }}
>
> The **cardinal successor** of an ordinal $\alpha$, denoted by $\alpha^+$, is the least cardinal greater than $\alpha$.
>
> The existence of $\alpha^+$ is justified by [CA#PROP-G](/codex/CA#PROP-G).
