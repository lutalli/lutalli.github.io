> {{ "EQU#DEF:Equinumerosity" | cod }}
>
> {:.no-indent}
> - $X$ and $Y$ are **equinumerous**, written
>
>   $$ X\equ Y, $$
>
>   if there exists a bijection from $X$ onto $Y$.
>
> - $X$ is **not a greater set** than $Y$, written
>
>   $$ X\lequ Y, $$
>
>   if there exists an injection from $X$ to $Y$.
>
> - $X$ is a **smaller set** than $Y$, written
>
>   $$ X\lnequ Y, $$
>
>   if
>
>   $$ X\lequ Y \enspace\land\enspace X\not\equ Y. $$

---

> {{ "EQU#PROP-REF:Reflexivity of $\equ$" | cod }}
>
> For any $X$,
>
> $$ X \equ X. $$

> {{ "EQU#PROP-SYM:Symmetry of $\equ$" | cod }}
>
> For any $X$ and $Y$,
>
> $$ X \equ Y \enspace\rimp\enspace Y \equ X. $$

> {{ "EQU#PROP-TRA:Transitivity of $\equ$" | cod }}
>
> For any $X$, $Y$ and $Z$,
>
> $$ X\equ Y \,\land\, Y\equ Z \enspace\rimp\enspace X\equ Z. $$

---

> {{ "EQU#PROP-EQV" | cod }}
>
> $\equ$ is an equivalence relation.

**Proof.**{:.run-in}By [reflexivity](#PROP-REF), [symmetry](#PROP-SYM) and [transitivity](#PROP-TRA) of $\equ$.<qed />

---

> {{ "EQU#PROP-L-REF:Reflexivity of $\lequ$" | cod }}
>
> For any $X$,
>
> $$ X \lequ X. $$

> {{ "EQU#PROP-L-TRA:Transitivity of $\lequ$" | cod }}
>
> For any $X$, $Y$ and $Z$,
>
> $$ X \lequ Y \,\land\, Y\lequ Z \enspace\rimp\enspace X\lequ Z. $$

> {{ "EQU#PROP-SB:Schröder-Bernstein Theorem" | cod }}
>
> For any $X$ and $Y$,
>
> $$ X \lequ Y \,\land\, Y\lequ X \enspace\rimp\enspace X\equ Y. $$

---


> {{ "EQU#PROP-SUB" | cod }}
>
> If $X\subseteq Y$, then $X\lequ Y$.

**Proof.**{:.run-in}$\id_X:X\to Y$ is an injection.<qed />
