> {{ "DEF-EQU:Equinumerosity" | codexify }}
>
> {:.no-indent}
> - $X$ and $Y$ are **equinumerous**, written $X\equ Y$, if there exists a bijection from $X$ onto $Y$.
>
> - $X\lequ Y$ if there exists an injection from $X$ to $Y$.
>
> - $X\lnequ Y$ if $X\lequ Y$ and $X\not\equ Y$.

---

> {{ "PROP-EQU-REF:Reflexivity of $\equ$" | codexify }}
>
> For any $X$,
>
> $$ X \equ X. $$

> {{ "PROP-EQU-SYM:Symmetry of $\equ$" | codexify }}
>
> For any $X$ and $Y$,
>
> $$ X\equ Y \enspace\rimp\enspace Y\equ X. $$

> {{ "PROP-EQU-TR:Transitivity of $\equ$" | codexify }}
>
> For any $X$, $Y$ and $Z$,
>
> $$ X\equ Y \,\land\, Y\equ Z \enspace\rimp\enspace X\equ Z. $$

As a result:

> {{ "PROP-EQU-EQV" | codexify }}
>
> $\equ$ is an equivalence relation.

---

> {{ "PROP-EQU-L-REF:Reflexivity of $\lequ$" | codexify }}
>
> For any $X$,
>
> $$ X\lequ X. $$

> {{ "PROP-EQU-L-TR:Transitivity of $\lequ$" | codexify }}
>
> For any $X$, $Y$ and $Z$,
>
> $$ X\lequ Y \,\land\, Y\lequ Z \enspace\rimp\enspace X\lequ Z. $$

> {{ "PROP-EQU-L-ATS:Antisymmetry of $\lequ$ (SchrĆ¶der-Bernstein Theorem)" | codexify }}
>
> For any $X$ and $Y$,
>
> $$ X\lequ Y \,\land\, Y\lequ X \enspace\rimp\enspace X\equ Y. $$

> {{ "PROP-EQU-L-CON:Connectivity of $\lequ$" | codexify }} <span class="adjunctive">$\lrimp\AC$</span>
>
> For any $X$ and $Y$,
>
> $$ X\lequ Y \enspace\lor\enspace Y\lequ X. $$

As a result:

> {{ "PROP-EQU-L-PO" | codexify }}
>
> $\lequ$ is a partial order. If $\AC$ is assumed, it is further a total order.

---

> {{ "PROP-EQU-SUB" | codexify }}
>
> If $X\subseteq Y$, then $X\lequ Y$.

**Proof.**{:.run-in}$\id_X:X\to Y$ is an injection.<qed />
