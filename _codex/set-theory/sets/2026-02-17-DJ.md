> {{ "DEF-DJ:Disjoint Sets" | codexify }}
>
> {:.no-indent}
> - $X$ and $Y$ are **disjoint** if $X\cap Y=\varnothing$.
>
> - The sets from $X$ are disjoint if $\bigcap X=\varnothing$.
>
> - Not disjoint sets are called **joint** / **overlapping**.

---

> {{ "PROP-DJ-EMP" | codexify }}
>
> $\varnothing$ is disjoint to all sets.
>
> ($\varnothing$ is disjoint to itself!)

> {{ "PROP-DJ-SUB" | codexify }}
>
> Let $X$, $Y$ be non-empty. If $X\subseteq Y$, then $X$ and $Y$ are joint.
