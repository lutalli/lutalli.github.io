> {{ "DEF-DJ:Disjoint Sets" | codexify }}
>
> {:.no-indent}
> - $X$ and $Y$ are **disjoint** if
>
>   $$ X\cap Y = \varnothing. $$
>
> - $X$ is a **disjoint collection** if
>
>   $$ \bigcap X = \varnothing. $$
>
> - Not disjoint sets are called **joint** / **overlapping**.

---

> {{ "PROP-DJ-SLF" | codexify }}
>
> $\varnothing$ is the only set disjoint to itself.

**Proof.**{:.run-out}

- $$ \varnothing\cap\varnothing = \varnothing. $$

- By [PROP-IT-IPO](/codex/IT#PROP-IT-IPO), if $X$ is disjoint to itself,

  $$ X = X\cap X = \varnothing. $$

&#8203;<qed />

> {{ "PROP-DJ-ALL" | codexify }}
>
> $\varnothing$ is the only set disjoint to any other set.

**Proof.**{:.run-out}

- $\varnothing$ is disjoint to any set by [PROP-IT-EMP](/codex/IT#PROP-IT-EMP).

- If $X$ is non-empty,

  $$ Y = X\cup\{X\} $$

  is always joint to $X$. By [PROP-ZF-SLF](/codex/ZF#PROP-ZF-SLF), $X\neq Y$.

&#8203;<qed />
