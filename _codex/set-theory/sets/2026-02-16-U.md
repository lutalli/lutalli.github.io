> {{ "DEF-U:Union" | codexify }}
>
> {:.no-indent}
> - The **union** of $X$, written as
>
>   $$ \bigcup X, $$
>
>   is the unique set $Y$ such that
>
>   $$ \forall y :\enspace y\in Y \enspace\leftrightarrow\enspace \exists x:x\in X\land y\in x. $$
>
>   The existence of $Y$ is justified by [Axiom of Union](/codex/ZF#AX-ZF-U) and the uniqueness is justified by [Axiom of Extensionality](/codex/ZF#AX-ZF-EXT).
>
> - We write
>
>   $$ \begin{align*}
        A\cup B &= \bigcup\{A,B\}, \\
        A\cup B\cup C &= (A\cup B)\cup C, \\
        A\cup B\cup C\cup D &= (A\cup B\cup C)\cup D
    \end{align*} $$
>
>   and so on.

> {{ "PROP-U-EMP" | codexify }}
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. $\displaystyle \bigcup\varnothing = \varnothing$.
>
> 2. $X\cup\varnothing = X$ for any $X$.

> {{ "PROP-U-IPO:Idempotence" | codexify }}
>
> For any $X$,
>
> $$ X\cup X = X. $$

> {{ "PROP-U-COM:Commutativity" | codexify }}
>
> For any $A$ and $B$,
>
> $$ A\cup B = B\cup A. $$

> {{ "PROP-U-ASS:Associativity" | codexify }}
>
> For any $A$, $B$ and $C$,
>
> $$ (A\cup B)\cup C = A\cup (B\cup C). $$

As a result:

> {{ "PROP-U-MO" | codexify }}
>
> For any set $X$, $(\powerset(X),\cup)$ is an [abelian](/codex/MAG#DEF-MAG-P) [monoid](/codex/MO) with neutral element $\varnothing$.
