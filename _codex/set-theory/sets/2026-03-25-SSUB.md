> {{ "DEF-SSUB:Sized Subset" | codexify }}
>
> {:.no-indent}
> - Let $X$ be a set and $\kappa\leq\lvert X\rvert$ be a [cardinal](/codex/CA). A **$\kappa$-sized subset** of $X$ is a [subset](codex/SUB) of $X$ whose [cardinality](/codex/CARD) is $\kappa$.
>
> - The set of all $\kappa$-sized subsets of $X$ is denoted by
>
>   $$ \powerset_\kappa(X) = \{A\subseteq X:\lvert A\rvert=\kappa\}. $$
