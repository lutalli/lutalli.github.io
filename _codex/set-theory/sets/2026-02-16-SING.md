> {{ "DEF-SING:Singleton" | codexify }}
>
> The **singleton** $\\{a\\}$ is the set $\\{a,a\\}$.

---

> {{ "PROP-SING-SLF" | codexify }}
>
> For any $X$,
>
> $$ X\neq\{X\}. $$

**Proof.**{:.run-in}If $X=\\{X\\}$, $X\in X$, contrary to [PROP-ZF-SLF](/codex/ZF#PROP-ZF-SLF).<qed />

> {{ "PROP-SING-CARD" | codexify }}
>
> For any $a$,
>
> $$ \big\lvert\{a\}\big\rvert = 1. $$

**Proof.**{:.run-out}

$$ \{(a,0)\} $$

is a bijection from $\{a\}$ to $1$.<qed />
