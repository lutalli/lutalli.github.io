> {{ "SUB#DEF:Subset" | cod }}
>
> {:.no-indent}
> - $X$ is a **subset** of $Y$, written
>
>   $$X\subseteq Y, $$
>
>   if
>
>   $$ \forall x :\enspace x\in X \enspace\rimp\enspace x\in Y. $$
> 
> - $X$ is a **proper subset** of $Y$, written
>
>   $$X\subset Y, $$
>
>   if
>
>   $$ X\subseteq Y \enspace\land\enspace X\neq Y. $$

---

> {{ "SUB#PROP-EMP" | cod }}
>
> $\varnothing$ is the only set that is a subset of any non-empty set.

**Proof.**{:.run-out}

- $\varnothing$ is a subset of any set since no $x\in\varnothing$.

- If $X$ is a subset of any non-empty set, then $X\subseteq\\{X\\}$. By [ZF#PROP-SLF](/codex/ZF#PROP-SLF), $X\neq\\{X\\}$, so $X=\varnothing$.

&#8203;<qed />

---

> {{ "SUB#PROP-REF:Reflexivity" | cod }}
>
> For any $X$,
>
> $$ X \subseteq X. $$

> {{ "SUB#PROP-TRA:Transitivity" | cod }}
>
> For any $X$, $Y$ and $Z$,
>
> $$ X\subseteq Y \,\land\, Y\subseteq Z \enspace\rimp\enspace X\subseteq Z. $$

> {{ "SUB#PROP-ATS:Antisymmetry" | cod }}
>
> For any $X$ and $Y$,
>
> $$ X\subseteq Y \,\land\, Y\subseteq X \enspace\rimp\enspace X=Y. $$

**Proof.**{:.run-in}By [Axiom of Extensionality](/codex/ZF#AX-EXT).<qed />

---

> {{ "SUB#PROP-PO" | cod }}
>
> $\subseteq$ is a partial order.

**Proof.**{:.run-in}By [reflexivity](#PROP-REF), [transitivity](#PROP-TRA) and [antisymmetry](#PROP-ATS) of $\subseteq$.<qed />
