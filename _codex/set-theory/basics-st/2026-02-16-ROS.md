> {{ "ROS#DEF:Roster Notation" | cod }}
>
> We write
>
> $$ \begin{align*}
      \{a,b,c\} &= \{a,b\}\cup\{c\}, \\
      \{a,b,c,d\} &= \{a,b,c\}\cup\{d\}, \\
      \{a,b,c,d,e\} &= \{a,b,c,d\}\cup\{e\}
  \end{align*} $$
>
> and so on.

- Synonym of *Roster Notation*: **Enumeration Notation**

---

> {{ "ROS#PROP" | cod }}
>
> For any $a_1$, $\cdots$, $a_n$ ($n\geq 1$),
>
> $$ \forall x :\enspace x\in\{a_1,\cdots,a_n\} \enspace\lrimp\enspace x=a_1\lor\cdots\lor x=a_n. $$
