> {{ "EMP#DEF:Empty Set" | cod }}
>
> The **empty set** $\varnothing$ is the unique set such that
>
> $$ \forall x : x\notin\varnothing. $$
>
> The existence of $\varnothing$ is justified by [Axiom of Empty Set](/codex/ZF#AX-EMP), [Axiom of Existence](/codex/ZF#AX-EX) or [Axiom of Infinity](/codex/ZF#AX-INF). Its uniqueness is justified by [Axiom of Extensionality](/codex/ZF#AX-EXT).

---

> {{ "EMP#PROP-CARD" | cod }}
>
> $$ \lvert\varnothing\rvert = 0. $$

**Proof.**{:.run-in}$\varnothing$ is a bijection from $\varnothing$ to $0$.<qed />
