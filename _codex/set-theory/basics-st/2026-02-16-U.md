> {{ "U#DEF:Union" | cod }}
>
> {:.no-indent}
> - The **union** of $X$, written as
>
>   $$ \bigcup X, $$
>
>   is the unique set $Y$ such that
>
>   $$ \forall y :\enspace y\in Y \enspace\lrimp\enspace \exists x:x\in X\land y\in x. $$
>
>   The existence of $Y$ is justified by [Axiom of Union](/codex/ZF#AX-U) and its uniqueness is justified by [Axiom of Extensionality](/codex/ZF#AX-EXT).
>
> - We write
>
>   $$ \begin{align*}
        A\cup B &= \bigcup\{A,B\}, \\
        A\cup B\cup C &= (A\cup B)\cup C, \\
        A\cup B\cup C\cup D &= (A\cup B\cup C)\cup D
    \end{align*} $$
>
>   and so on.

> {{ "U#PROP-EMP" | cod }}
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. $$ \bigcup\varnothing = \varnothing. $$
>
> 2. For any $X$,
>
>    $$ X\cup\varnothing = X. $$

> {{ "U#PROP-IDP:Idempotence" | cod }}
>
> For any $X$,
>
> $$ X\cup X = X. $$

> {{ "U#PROP-COM:Commutativity" | cod }}
>
> For any $X$ and $Y$,
>
> $$ X\cup Y = Y\cup X. $$

> {{ "U#PROP-ASS:Associativity" | cod }}
>
> For any $X$, $Y$ and $Z$,
>
> $$ (X\cup Y)\cup Z = X\cup(Y\cup Z). $$

> {{ "U#PROP-MO" | cod }}
>
> $(\V,\cup)$ is an abelian monoid with neutral element $\varnothing$.

**Proof.**{:.run-in}By [commutativity](#PROP-COM), [associativity](#PROP-ASS) of $\cup$ and [U#PROP-EMP (II)](#PROP-EMP).<qed />
