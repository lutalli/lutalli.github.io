> {{ "SING#DEF:Singleton" | cod }}
>
> The **singleton** $\\{a\\}$ is the set $\\{a,a\\}$.

---

> {{ "SING#PROP-SLF" | cod }}
>
> For any $X$,
>
> $$ X \neq \{X\}. $$

**Proof.**{:.run-in}If $X=\\{X\\}$, $X\in X$, contrary to [ZF#PROP-SLF](/codex/ZF#PROP-SLF).<qed />

> {{ "SING#PROP-CARD" | cod }}
>
> For any $a$,
>
> $$ \big\lvert\{a\}\big\rvert = 1. $$

**Proof.**{:.run-in}$\\{(a,0)\\}$ is a bijection from $\\{a\\}$ to $1$.<qed />
