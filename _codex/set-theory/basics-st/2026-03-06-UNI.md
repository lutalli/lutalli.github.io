> {{ "UNI#DEF:Universal Class" | cod }}
>
> The **universal class** / **universe** is the class of all sets:
>
> $$ \V = \{x:x=x\}. $$

---

> {{ "UNI#PROP-PC" | cod }}
>
> $\V$ is a proper class.

**Proof.**{:.run-in}Since $\V=\V$, $\V\in\V$, contrary to [ZF#PROP-SLF](/codex/ZF#PROP-SLF).<qed />
