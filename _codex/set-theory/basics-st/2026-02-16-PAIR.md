> {{ "PAIR#DEF:Pair" | cod }}
>
> The **pair** of $a$ and $b$, written as
>
> $$ \{a,b\}, $$
>
> is the unique set $X$ such that
>
> $$ \forall x :\enspace x\in X \enspace\lrimp\enspace x=a\lor x=b. $$
>
> The existence of $X$ is justified by [Axiom of Pairing](/codex/ZF#AX-PAIR) and its uniqueness is justified by [Axiom of Extensionality](/codex/ZF#AX-EXT).

> {{ "PAIR#PROP-UO:Unorderedness" | cod }}
>
> For any $a$ and $b$,
>
> $$ \{a,b\} = \{b,a\}. $$

> {{ "PAIR#PROP-CARD" | cod }}
>
> For any $a\neq b$,
>
> $$ \big\lvert\{a,b\}\big\rvert = 2. $$

**Proof.**{:.run-in}$\\{(a,0),(b,1)\\}$ is a bijection from $\\{a,b\\}$ to $2$.<qed />
