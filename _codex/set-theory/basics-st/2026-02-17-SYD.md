> {{ "SYD#DEF:Symmetric Difference" | cod }}
>
> The **symmetric difference** between $X$ and $Y$ is
>
> $$ X\symd Y = (X\smallsetminus Y)\cup(Y\smallsetminus X). $$

---

> {{ "SYD#PROP-EMP" | cod }}
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. For any $X$,
>
>    $$ X\symd\varnothing = X. $$
>
> 2. For any $X$ and $Y$,
>
>    $$ X\symd Y = \varnothing \enspace\lrimp\enspace X = Y. $$

> {{ "SYD#PROP-COM:Commutativity" | cod }}
>
> For any $X$ and $Y$,
>
> $$ X\symd Y = Y\symd X. $$

> {{ "SYD#PROP-ASS:Associativity" | cod }}
>
> For any $X$, $Y$ and $Z$,
>
> $$ (X\symd Y)\symd Z = X\symd(Y\symd Z). $$

---

> {{ "SYD#PROP-GRP" | cod }}
>
> $(\V,\symd)$ is an abelian group with neutral element $\varnothing$.

**Proof.**{:.run-in}By [commutativity](#PROP-COM), [associativity](#PROP-ASS) and [SYD#PROP-EMP](#PROP-EMP).<qed />

---

> {{ "SYD#PROP-D" | cod }}
>
> For any $X$ and $Y$,
>
> $$ X\symd Y = (X\cup Y)\smallsetminus (X\cap Y). $$
