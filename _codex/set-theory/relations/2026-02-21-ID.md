> {{ "DEF-ID" | codexify }}
>
> The **identity** on $X$ is the binary relation
>
> $$ \id_X = \{(x,x):x\in X\}. $$

> {{ "PROP-ID-F" | codexify }}
>
> For any $X$, $\id_X$ is also a function.

---

> {{ "PROP-ID-EMP" | codexify }}
>
> $$ \id_\varnothing = \varnothing. $$
