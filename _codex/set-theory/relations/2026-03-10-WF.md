> {{ "WF#DEF:Well-Founded Relation" | cod }}
>
> A binary relation $\sim$ on $X$ is **well-founded** if every non-empty subset $A\subseteq X$ has a $\sim$-minimal element, i.e. an element $a\in A$ such that
>
> $$ \forall x\in A : x\not\sim a. $$
