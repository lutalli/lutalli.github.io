> {{ "RST#DEF:Restriction" | cod }}
>
> Let $R$ be a binary relation. If $A\subseteq\dom R$, the **left-restriction** / **restriction** of $R$ to $A$ is
>
> $$ {R\restriction_A} = \{(x,y):x\,R\,y\land x\in A\}. $$
>
> If $B\subseteq\ran R$, the **right-restriction** of $R$ to $B$ is
>
> $$ {R\restriction^B} = \{(x,y):x\,R\,y\land y\in B\}. $$
>
> These are sets:
>
> $$ {R\restriction_A}\subseteq R, \quad {R\restriction^B}\subseteq R. $$
