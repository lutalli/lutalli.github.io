In the following, addition, multiplication and exponentiation of [ordinals](/codex/ORD) are defined using [Transfinite Recursion](/codex/TREC).

> {{ "DEF-OAR-ADD:Addition" | codexify }}
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. $\alpha+0=\alpha$;
> 2. $\alpha+(\beta+1)=(\alpha+\beta)+1$;
> 3. $\alpha+\beta=\lim_{\xi\to\beta}(\alpha+\xi)$ for [limit ordinals](/codex/LO) $\beta$.

> {{ "DEF-OAR-MUL:Multiplication" | codexify }}
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. $\alpha\cdot 0=\alpha$;
> 2. $\alpha\cdot(\beta+1)=\alpha\cdot\beta+\alpha$;
> 3. $\alpha\cdot\beta=\lim_{\xi\to\beta}\alpha\cdot\xi$ for limit ordinals $\beta$.

> {{ "DEF-OAR-EXP:Exponentiation" | codexify }}
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. $\alpha^0=1$;
> 2. $\alpha^{\beta+1}=\alpha^\beta\cdot\alpha$;
> 3. $\alpha^\beta=\lim_{\xi\to\beta}\alpha^\xi$ for limit ordinals $\beta$.

---

> {{ "PROP-OAR-ASS:Associativities" | codexify }}
>
> For any ordinals $\alpha$, $\beta$ and $\gamma$:
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. $\alpha+(\beta+\gamma)=(\alpha+\beta)+\gamma$.
> 2. $\alpha\cdot(\beta\cdot\gamma)=(\alpha\cdot\beta)\cdot\gamma$.

> {{ "REM-OAR-COM" | codexify }}
>
> Neither $+$ or $\cdot$ is commutative:
>
> $$ 1+\omega=\omega\neq\omega+1, \quad 2\cdot\omega=\omega\neq\omega\cdot 2. $$
