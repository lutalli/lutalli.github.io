In the following, addition, multiplication and exponentiation of ordinals are defined using Transfinite Recursion.

> {{ "DEF-OAR-ADD:Addition" | codexify }}
>
> For any ordinal $\alpha$,
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. $$ \alpha+0 = \alpha; $$
>
> 2. $$ \alpha+(\beta+1) = (\alpha+\beta)+1 $$
>
>    for non-limit ordinals $\beta$;
>
> 3. $$ \alpha+\beta = \lim_{\xi\to\beta}(\alpha+\xi) $$
>
>    for limit ordinals $\beta$.

> {{ "DEF-OAR-MUL:Multiplication" | codexify }}
>
> For any ordinal $\alpha$,
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. $$ \alpha\cdot 0 = \alpha; $$
>
> 2. $$ \alpha\cdot(\beta+1) = \alpha\cdot\beta+\alpha $$
>
>    for non-limit ordinals $\beta$;
>
> 3. $$ \alpha\cdot\beta = \lim_{\xi\to\beta}\alpha\cdot\xi $$
>
>    for limit ordinals $\beta$.

> {{ "DEF-OAR-EXP:Exponentiation" | codexify }}
>
> For any ordinal $\alpha$,
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. $$ \alpha^0 = 1; $$
>
> 2. $$ \alpha^{\beta+1} = \alpha^\beta\cdot\alpha $$
>
>    for non-limit ordinals $\beta$;
>
> 3. $$ \alpha^\beta = \lim_{\xi\to\beta}\alpha^\xi $$
>
>    for limit ordinals $\beta$.

---

> {{ "PROP-OAR-ADD-ASS:Associativity of $+$" | codexify }}
>
> For any ordinals $\alpha$, $\beta$ and $\gamma$,
>
> $$ \alpha+(\beta+\gamma) = (\alpha+\beta)+\gamma. $$
>

> {{ "PROP-OAR-MUL-ASS:Associativity of $\cdot$" | codexify }}
>
> For any ordinals $\alpha$, $\beta$ and $\gamma$,
>
> $$ \alpha\cdot(\beta\cdot\gamma) = (\alpha\cdot\beta)\cdot\gamma. $$

> {{ "REM-OAR-COM" | codexify }}
>
> Neither $+$ or $\cdot$ is commutative:
>
> $$ 1+\omega=\omega\neq\omega+1, \quad 2\cdot\omega=\omega\neq\omega\cdot 2. $$
