> {{ "IDC#PROP:Induction" | cod }}
>
> Let $A$ be a subset of $\N$. Suppose:
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. $$ 0 \in A. $$
>
> 2. $$ \forall n\in A : n+1\in A. $$
>
> Then $A=\N$.

**Proof.**{:.run-in}Otherwise, $\min\N\setminus A$ would be a limit ordinal, contrary to [N#PROP-LO](/codex/N#PROP-LO).<qed />
