> {{ "PROP-TREC:Transfinite Recursion" | codexify }}
>
> If $G$ is a [function](/codex/F) on $\V$, then there is a unique function $F$ on $\Ord$ such that
>
> $$ F(\alpha) = G(F\restriction_\alpha) $$
>
> for every $\alpha\in\Ord$.
