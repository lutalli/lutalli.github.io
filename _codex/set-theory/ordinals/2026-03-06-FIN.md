> {{ "DEF-FIN:Finite and Infinite Set" | codexify }}
>
> {:.no-indent}
> - $X$ is **finite** if there is a [bijection](/codex/BIJ) from $X$ onto a [natural number](/codex/N), i.e.
>
>   $$ \lvert X\rvert = n $$
>
>   for some $n\in\N$.
>
> - $X$ is **infinite** if it is not finite.
