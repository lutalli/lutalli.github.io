> {{ "FIN#DEF:Finite and Infinite Set" | cod }}
>
> {:.no-indent}
> - $X$ is **finite** if there is a bijection from $X$ to a natural number, i.e. for some $n\in\N$,
>
>   $$ \lvert X\rvert = n. $$
>
> - $X$ is **infinite** if it is not finite.
