> {{ "DEF-FIN:Finite and Infinite Set" | codexify }}
>
> {:.no-indent}
> - $X$ is **finite** if there is a bijection from $X$ to a natural number, i.e.
>
>   $$ \lvert X\rvert = n $$
>
>   for some $n\in\N$.
>
> - $X$ is **infinite** if it is not finite.
