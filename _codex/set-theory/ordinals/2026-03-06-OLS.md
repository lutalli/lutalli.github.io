> {{ "DEF-OLS:Limit of Sequence" | codexify }}
>
> Let $\alpha$ be a [limit ordinal](/codex/LO) and let $\langle\gamma_\xi\rangle_{\xi<\alpha}$ be an [increasing](/codex/OH) [sequence](/codex/SEQ) of ordinals. The **limit** of $\gamma$ is
>
> $$ \lim_{\xi\to\alpha}\gamma_\xi = \sup\{\gamma_\xi:\xi<\alpha\}. $$
