>
> Let $\alpha$ be a limit ordinal and let $\gamma$ be an increasing $\alpha$-sequence of ordinals. The **limit** of $\gamma$ is
>
> $$ \lim_{\xi\to\alpha}\gamma_\xi = \sup\{\gamma_\xi:\xi<\alpha\}. $$
