> {{ "PROP-TIDC:Transfinite Induction" | codexify }}
>
> Let $C$ be a [class](/codex/CLS) of [ordinals](/codex/ORD). Suppose
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. $0\in C$;
> 2. $\forall \alpha\in C:\alpha+1\in C$;
> 3. $\alpha\subset C \rimp \alpha\in C$ for every [limit ordinal](/codex/LO) $\alpha$.
>
> Then $C=\Ord$.
