> {{ "TIDC#PROP:Transfinite Induction" | cod }}
>
> Let $C$ be a class of ordinals. Suppose:
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. $$ 0\in C. $$
> 2. $$ \forall\alpha\in C : \alpha+1\in C. $$
> 3. For every limit ordinal $\alpha$,
>
>    $$ \alpha\subseteq C \enspace\rimp\enspace \alpha\in C. $$
>
> Then $C=\Ord$.
