> {{ "INJ#DEF:Injection" | cod }}
>
> {:.no-indent}
> - An **injective function** / **injection** is a left-unique function, i.e. a function $f:X\to Y$ such that 
>
>   $$ \forall x,y\in X :\enspace f(x)=f(y) \enspace\rimp\enspace x=y. $$
>
> - The set of all injections from $X$ to $Y$ is denoted by
>
>   $$ \inj(X,Y). $$

**Examples.**{:.run-out}

1. The empty function $\varnothing$ is an injection.
