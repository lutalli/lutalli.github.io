> {{ "BIJ#DEF:Bijection" | cod }}
>
> {:.no-indent}
> - A **bijective function** / **bijection** is a function that is both injective and surjective.
>
> - The set of all bijections from $X$ onto $Y$ is denoted by
>
>   $$ \bij(X,Y). $$

- Synonym of *bijection*: **One-to-one correspondence**
