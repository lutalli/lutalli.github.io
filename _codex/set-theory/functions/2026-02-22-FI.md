> {{ "DEF-FI:Inverse Function" | codexify }}
>
> Let $f$ be a function. If $f$ is injective, then the converse $f^{-1}$ is also a function and is called the **inverse** of $f$.

---

> {{ "PROP-FI-ID" | codexify }}
>
> For any injection $f$:
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. $$ f\circ f^{-1} = \id_{\ran f}. $$
>
> 2. $$ f^{-1}\circ f = \id_{\dom f}. $$
