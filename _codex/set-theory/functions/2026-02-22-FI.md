> {{ "FI#DEF:Inverse Function" | cod }}
>
> Let $f$ be an injection, then the converse $f^{-1}$ is also a function and is called the **inverse** of $f$.

---

> {{ "FI#PROP-ID" | cod }}
>
> For any injection $f$:
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. $$ f\circ f^{-1} = \id_{\ran f}. $$
>
> 2. $$ f^{-1}\circ f = \id_{\dom f}. $$
