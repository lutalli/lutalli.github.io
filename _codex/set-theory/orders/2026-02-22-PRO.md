> {{ "DEF-PRO:Preorder" | codexify }}
>
> {:.no-indent}
> - A **preorder** is a binary relation that is reflexive and transitive.
>
> - If $\preceq$ is a preorder, then $\prec$ refers to the relation defined by
>
>   $$ x\prec y \enspace\lrimp\enspace x\preceq y\land x\neq y. $$

> {{ "REM-PRO-S" | codexify }}
>
> There is no "strict preorder", i.e. a binary relation that is irreflexive and transitive, because such a relation is automatically asymmetric, making it a strict partial order. See [PROP-PO-ASY](/codex/PO#PROP-PO-ASY).
