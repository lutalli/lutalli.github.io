> {{ "DEF-GR:Greatest and Least Element" | codexify }}
>
> Let $(X,\preceq)$ be a [preordered set](/codex/PRO) and $a\in X$.
>
> - $a$ is a **greatest element** of $X$ if
>
>   $$ \forall x\in X : x\preceq a. $$
>
> - $a$ is a **least element** of $Y$ if
>
>   $$ \forall x\in X : a\preceq x. $$

---

> {{ "PROP-GR-MA" | codexify }}
>
> Let $(X,\preceq)$ be a [preordered set](/codex/PRO) and $a\in X$.
>
> - If $a$ is greatest, then it is [maximal](/codex/MA).
> - If $a$ is least, then it is minimal.
