> {{ "GR#DEF:Greatest and Least Element" | cod }}
>
> Let $X$ be a preordered set and $a\in X$.
>
> {:.no-indent}
> - $a$ is a **greatest element** of $X$ if for all $x\in X$,
>
>   $$ x \leq a. $$
>
> - $a$ is a **least element** of $X$ if for all $x\in X$,
>
>   $$ a \leq x. $$

---

> {{ "GR#PROP-MA" | cod }}
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. Any greatest element is maximal.
> 2. Any least element is minimal.
