> {{ "LM#DEF:Linear Mapping" | cod }}
>
> Let $V$, $W$ be two vector spaces over $F$. A function $f:V\to W$ is a **linear mapping** / **vector space homomorphism** if:
>
> {:.par-upper-roman}
> 1. *Additivity.* For all $x$, $y\in V$,
>
>    $$ f(x + y) = f(x) + f(y). $$
>
> 2. *Homogeneity.* For all $x\in V$ and $\lambda\in F$,
>
>    $$ f(\lambda x) = \lambda f(x). $$
